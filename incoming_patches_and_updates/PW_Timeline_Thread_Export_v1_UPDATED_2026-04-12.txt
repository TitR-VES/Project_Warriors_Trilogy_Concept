SECTION 2 — CANONICAL DATE ANCHORS (CONFIRMED)
================================================================================

The following dates are confirmed canonical anchors for the entire trilogy:

September 3, 2028
  - PANDORA attacks. Nationwide US cyber attack.
  - West Coast amphibious invasion begins.
  - Russia opens Baltic front.
  - China invades Taiwan.
  - Spook confronts Goldberg on East McFarlane Island simultaneously.
  - First reported: 1033 PST by local media.

November 3, 2028
  - US Presidential Election (suspended by Crodyn via SCOTUS ruling under wartime
    emergency provisions).
  - Exactly two months after PANDORA attacks.

June 4, 2028
  - Glushkin activation by Lebedev/Nasmith at Specter Aerospace, Peabody MA.
  - 91 days before PANDORA attacks.

Lebedev activation: Approximately 3 months before PANDORA (early June 2028).

Game 2 Romeo campaign opens: Approximately Day 31 of Calloway occupation.
  - Day 1 of Calloway occupation = September 3, 2028 (PANDORA day).
  - Day 31 = approximately October 4, 2028.
  - This is the approximate Game 2 campaign open date.

Crodyn's wartime presidential address: Late October or very early November 2028.
  - Falls within days of the suspended election date (November 3).
  - This is the convergent event connecting all three Game 2 campaigns.

Inter-game time gap (Game 1 to Game 2): NOT YET LOCKED. [FLAGGED]
  - Spook and Ducky campaign openings reference "six weeks post-PANDORA."
  - Romeo's "Day 31 of occupation" puts his campaign open at approximately October 4.
  - These are approximately coherent but not formally reconciled.
  - Resolution needed before Game 3 development deepens.

SECTION 3 — GAME 2 GLOBAL STRATEGIC CONTEXT
================================================================================

Setting: Continuation of global conflict immediately following Game 1.

United States:
  - Russian occupation of US East Coast and interior.
  - Calloway, KY (and surrounding Harlan County region) under occupation.
  - US Air Force partially degraded by PANDORA cyber effects.
  - Network-dependent aircraft (F-22, F-35) compromised or non-operational.
  - Legacy platforms (A-10 Warthog) operationally relevant due to PANDORA resilience
    and Crodyn administration's military mismanagement.
  - AFC (American Freedom Corps) emerging as organized resistance movement.
  - UNHMNA (United Nations Humanitarian Mission to North America) active:
    White UN vehicles, blue helmets, foreign troops in American environments.
    Hospital ships Dawn of Mercy and Beacon of Hope.
    Jarring visual juxtaposition — imagery historically associated with
    developing-world conflicts now in American suburban landscapes.

Europe:
  - Russian Baltic offensive ongoing.
  - NATO Allied Rapid Reaction forces active across northern/eastern Europe.
  - DAOG (multi-national command structure) operational.
  - AEGIS Squad (Ducky) running EW interdiction campaign across Baltic states and Arctic.

Asia-Pacific:
  - China has annexed Taiwan.
  - China moving on South Asia and Middle East.
  - South Pacific front opening (Australia theater beginning).

Convergent Event — Crodyn's Presidential Address:
  - Crodyn names AFC as domestic terrorist organization under 18 USC.
  - Names LT Ramón Álvarez by name as rogue officer.
  - Claims AFC coordinates with foreign intelligence.
  - 30-day voluntary disengagement window announced.
  - Framed as protecting wartime constitutional order.
  - Affirmed by SCOTUS Justices Howard (fictionalized Clarence Thomas)
    and Pedilla (fictionalized Samuel Alito).
  - Full address text written and available in sidebar thread.
  - Structural lynchpin connecting all three Game 2 campaigns.
