SECTION 1: CHINA STRATEGIC ARCHITECTURE
============================================================

CONFIRMED — China's Role Across the Trilogy

China's silence following Crodyn's false-flag accusation is explained by active consolidation,
not innocence. They did not orchestrate Riptide Red. They read it correctly while everyone
else was still inside it, let the conspiracy run, quietly harvested intelligence access through
the chaos, and executed their own strategy at the moment of maximum Western vulnerability.
This makes China a fundamentally different kind of adversary from Russia or the domestic
conspirators — patient, structural, and opportunistic rather than ideologically driven or
emotionally reactive.

China's strategic actions during and after the PANDORA attacks:
- Annexed Taiwan, seizing control of strategic chip foundry infrastructure and monopolizing
  access to advanced semiconductor manufacturing
- Moved on Pine Gap (Australia) to neutralize the signals intelligence and satellite relay
  facility — this is the primary strategic rationale for the Australian military operation
- Attacked the Torrejón de Ardoz AEGIS facility using unmarked operators (previously attributed
  to Russia — now confirmed as Chinese, change to be implemented in Game 2 Ducky campaign)
- Supported Russian operations against AEGIS and other Western infrastructure
- Made moves in the Middle East and South Asia during the window of Western vulnerability
- Exploited persistent access to NATO and AEGIS operational infrastructure data, acquired
  through a combination of intelligence operations, theft from Russian intelligence, and
  long-running MSS technical penetration operations

CONFIRMED — The Supply Chain Ceiling

China's Taiwan annexation and chip foundry monopoly does not result in an unrecoverable
situation for the West, but it severely constrains the pace of recovery. The US and Europe
can manufacture chips and theoretically rebuild AEGIS and defensive infrastructure, but not
fast enough and not under active wartime conditions. Europe's only precision lithography
machine (the ASML facility in the Netherlands, already seeded in Ducky's Game 2 campaign)
is the single most critical bottleneck in any Western recovery effort. The dramatic premise
of Game 3 is not "can they rebuild at all" but "can they buy enough time and breathing room
to rebuild before the window closes permanently."

CONFIRMED — The False Flag Consumed by a Bigger Conspiracy

Riptide Red was a controlled domestic coup. Foreign partners agreed to a show-of-force
invasion sufficient to justify Crodyn's domestic emergency framing, then withdraw once his
unconstitutional third term was secured. What the conspirators failed to account for was
that prior corruption had gutted FEMA and domestic response infrastructure, making the
PANDORA attacks hit harder and spread further than modeled. Crodyn, cut off by the
communications failure and operating in a delusional bunker mentality, believed he was
consolidating power. His partners, watching from outside the chaos, saw weakness and
opportunity and reneged on the withdrawal agreement. This is the structural hinge between
Games 1 and 2. China's separate agenda and Russia's opportunism fully detached from the
original conspiracy architecture at this moment. The false flag worked and failed
simultaneously — it achieved Crodyn's domestic goals but triggered a real war nobody in
the Riptide Red circle actually wanted.

When Crodyn realized the deal was dead, he turned on Goldberg. Goldberg, recognizing that
a panicking Crodyn made him the most dangerous variable in the system, made a preemptive
move: surrender on a trivial classified information charge, take a lenient plea, and
disappear through Coleman Federal Prison before Crodyn could have him killed or genuinely
exposed. The fake death via the Bisutri operation gives Goldberg official nonexistence —
making him simultaneously untouchable and irrelevant to the powers that would otherwise
pursue him.

CONFIRMED — Seeding Requirements (China)

Game 1: A PYTHIA anomaly flag — Jaeger notes that Chinese signals intelligence went
unusually quiet immediately after the PANDORA attacks. Not an accusation. Just an anomaly
that gets filed away and only makes sense in retrospect. This is the minimum viable China
seeding for the first game.

Game 2: The Torrejón mission pivot (unmarked Chinese operators replacing Russian attribution).
Wei Junlong's two indirect appearances (see Section 2). The Taiwan chip foundry consequence
made explicit somewhere in Ducky's storyline. The ASML Netherlands facility as a load-bearing
mission asset connecting to the supply chain thread.

PROTECTED DRAMATIC ASSET — The scene in which a character (Goldberg as the natural voice)
articulates the false-flag-consumed-by-a-bigger-conspiracy irony. The moment where someone
who was inside the conspiracy acknowledges with bitter clarity that they built the door that
let in something they could not control. This scene should be protected and placed carefully —
probably Spook's Game 2 or Game 3 campaign, after sufficient player understanding has
accumulated. Do not deploy early.

SECTION 2: WEI JUNLONG — CHARACTER PROFILE (WORKING DRAFT)
============================================================

STATUS: First draft confirmed. Not yet fully canonical. Subject to revision.

Name: Wei Junlong (魏俊龙)
Cover Identity: Jonathan Wei, Senior Regional Director, GenCorp Asia-Pacific
  Infrastructure Division, Singapore
Agency: MSS Technical Reconnaissance Bureau, Third Department
Internal Designation: PAVILION

Physical Profile:
Male. Early to mid forties. Lean, composed bearing — stillness that reads as either
patience or danger depending on context. Well-dressed without ostentation. Looks entirely
at home in a boardroom, a diplomatic reception, or the back of a government vehicle.
No visible indicators of field background. Deliberate.

Background:
Born Chengdu, Sichuan Province, 1983. Father: PLA signals engineer. Mother: university
lecturer in international law. Educated at Peking University (computer science and
international relations), postgraduate at University of Edinburgh. English is native-register
with a faint Scottish cadence he has never entirely shed and occasionally deploys deliberately
to put Westerners at ease. Recruited by MSS Technical Reconnaissance Bureau at 26 following
a brief career at a state-affiliated telecommunications company (MSS front in practice).
Cover as Jonathan Wei constructed over more than a decade. Area of expertise: the
intersection of commercial data infrastructure and Western defense communications architecture.

Function in Story:
Wei is the human face of China's patient, structural approach to the conflict. Not a soldier.
Not an ideologue. A systems thinker who has spent fifteen years mapping Western
vulnerabilities from inside Western institutions. Appears in Spook's campaign and Ducky's
campaign from different angles — neither recognizes him as a primary target until Game 3.

Moral Register:
Morally complex professional. Serves China's interests with genuine competence and without
visible crisis of conscience about most of what he does. Understands the consequences of
what China has set in motion — that a world structurally dependent on a single power's
goodwill for technological survival tends toward one historically predictable outcome. Has
concluded this is not his problem to solve. There is one fracture line: a long-standing
professional relationship with a Taiwanese semiconductor engineer (former Edinburgh
colleague, now at TSMC) whose situation after the annexation is something Wei does not
discuss and visibly does not want discussed. Whether this fracture opens into something
actionable is a Game 3 question.

PROTECTED STRUCTURAL ASSET — The Taiwanese engineer is the likely architect of the
TETHERBREAK credential kill switch. Their foundry/firmware knowledge makes them the
person who can reverse-engineer PANDORA's persistence vulnerability. Wei's personal
fracture and the technical resolution of PANDORA may be the same thread. Treat as
load-bearing and do not resolve casually.

Voice and Manner:
Measured. Precise. Genuinely curious about people in the way a very good intelligence
officer is curious — not warmly, but attentively. Rarely volunteers information but responds
to direct questions with a candor that feels surprising and is probably calculated. Does not
threaten. Does not posture. When he tells you something will happen, it is because he has
already ensured it will. In conversation with Spook he is the first antagonist-adjacent
character who treats Spook as a genuine peer rather than an obstacle or asset.

PROTECTED QUOTE (Wei Junlong):
"The Russians wanted an empire. The Chinese wanted the world."
— Deploy late Game 3, after the player has accumulated sufficient understanding of who
Wei is and what China has done. Either in direct conversation with Spook or as a recovered
private document. Never deploy early. The line must be earned.

Seeding Schedule (Confirmed):
- Game 1: Name only. One mention in a corporate filing or conference attendee list in a
  collectible. Nothing underlined.
- Game 2: Two appearances. (1) A flagged travel anomaly in Jaeger's PYTHIA report — Wei's
  pattern in the weeks before PANDORA placed him in proximity to three AEGIS-adjacent
  facilities under cover of GenCorp site inspections. Nothing actionable; just a pattern
  that doesn't fit. (2) Background GenCorp presence in the Abu Dhabi mission — possibly
  glimpsed, possibly a voice on an intercepted call, never identified by name in that moment.
- Game 3: Full presence. The distance closes.

SECTION 3: GAME 3 CAMPAIGN CONCEPTS — EARLY SCRATCHPAD
============================================================

All items in this section are early-stage concepts. None are fully developed or canonically
locked. They are held here for future development passes.

--- 3A: OCCUPIED BOSTON CAMPAIGN ---

STATUS: Parked for development.

One of the Game 3 campaigns will be set in occupied Boston. Urban warfare in dense
historical terrain — architecturally and culturally distinct from Harlan County, Kentucky,
providing immediate tonal contrast with Romeo's Game 2 American Freedom campaign. Boston
offers colonial-era underground infrastructure, a major port, significant symbolic resonance
as a city with its own revolutionary history, and the deliberate thematic inversion of
British forces in Boston now reimagined through a Russian occupation lens (see UK subplot
note in Section 3F below). The West Coast was the original amphibious invasion beachhead.
Boston represents a different theater — the Eastern seaboard occupation front.

--- 3B: NEW AMERICA ACCELERATIONIST FACTION ---

STATUS: Speculative. Parked for development.

A far-right domestic extremist faction drawing from Three Percenters, Boogaloo Boys, and
accelerationist movements. Initially ambiguous during the Russian occupation arc — possible
grudging co-belligerents against the occupation before revealing themselves as something
more dangerous. Their ideology: the invasion and collapse of federal authority is not a
catastrophe to be reversed but an opportunity — a controlled demolition of the old order
to clear ground for something new. They don't want to liberate America. They want to reset it.

Nuclear escalation thread (speculative):
Two possible expressions, both undeveloped:
(1) Provoke a nuclear exchange — engineer a situation forcing one of the warring powers to
launch, triggering civilizational collapse they believe they can survive and inherit.
(2) Self-detonation — gain access to US nuclear arsenal assets (possibly through
TETHERBREAK-adjacent infrastructure compromise or corruption of surviving command-and-control
elements) and trigger a limited domestic detonation. Logic: if you can't trust the system
to die cleanly, you kill it yourself.

Dale Pruett connection: Speculative and unconfirmed. Possible shapes include: Dale has
pre-occupation history with the movement without Romeo's knowledge; Dale's network
unknowingly overlaps with cells that have been infiltrated or co-opted; or Dale becomes a
tragic figure pulled between AFC liberation goals and accelerationist nihilism.
This connection is deliberately left unresolved for future development.

Thematic note:
Game 1 enemy: the deep state and its foreign enablers.
Game 2 enemy: foreign occupation and institutional betrayal.
Game 3 enemy (potential): the idea that America should be saved at all.

--- 3C: QUEER CHARACTER IN AMERICAN FREEDOM CAMPAIGN ---

STATUS: Resolved. Character locked as Patrick "Pat" Napier.

Romeo's American Freedom campaign now includes Patrick "Pat" Napier, a trans
man and former U.S. Army enlisted 68W combat medic (one enlistment, former
SPC/E-4) back in the Calloway area as a civilian when the occupation begins.
Pat functions first as a capable adult, field medic, sustainment specialist,
and secondary rifleman inside Romeo's early resistance cell. His transness is
part of his history of institutional betrayal under tightening Crodyn-era
policy, but it is not his only or even primary scene-to-scene function.

The Kentucky setting remains load-bearing: Pat is written as someone who
already understands how visibility, privacy, and trust work in a small place.
That local texture matters. The occupation adds specific additional danger, but
the character should not be reduced to victimization. The betrayal axis is
primarily institutional/state/military rather than familial; his parents remain
supportive, and Renata Voss's support is best shown through natural adult
respect and trust rather than speeches.

Operational role in campaign:
- early resistance-cell casualty stabilizer / field medic / sustainment anchor
- secondary rifleman who creates space and buys treatment time
- adult counterweight to younger, more impulsive cell energy, especially Cody

--- 3D: SORAYA PROBST ---


STATUS: Concept confirmed. Character profile sketched.

Name: Soraya Probst
Background: First-generation German-Iranian. Father German (hence surname). Mother Iranian.
Placement: Ducky-adjacent character — intel team, AEGIS support, or similar non-squad role.
Game placement: Game 2 or Game 3 (undecided).

Function: Soraya is a lens for examining the cultural and geopolitical tensions surrounding
Iran's role as a Russian-aligned invasion force, and the overlapping real-world tensions
between Western and Islamic-world identities. She is not a commentary or a resolution — she
is a vessel through which these tensions become human and specific rather than abstract.

Character notes:
A German father and Iranian mother means Soraya grew up watching her mother navigate a
Western European society with its own complicated relationship to Iranian and broader Muslim
immigrant communities. She has been code-switching and mediating between worlds since
childhood as simple survival. Her relationship to Iran is matrilineal — her mother's country,
her mother's culture, potentially her mother's religion. That tends to produce a more
emotionally charged connection. Her German surname is partly what opened institutional doors
for her — a fact she is aware of and has complicated feelings about. The story does not need
to resolve the tensions she embodies. It needs to let them be present and real.

--- 3E: RUSSIAN-AMERICAN FAMILY (BOSTON) ---

STATUS: Concept confirmed. Character(s) undeveloped.

One Russian-American family — not a community portrait, one family — introduced in the
occupied Boston campaign. Maximum three generations if possible, with enough internal
difference of perspective to carry the lens function without becoming a symposium.

Suggested generational structure:
- Elderly first-generation figure who left the Soviet Union and has built a life here for
  decades — the invasion forces a reckoning with everything that act of emigrating meant.
- American-born middle generation — culturally hybrid in the way second-generation immigrants
  are, fluent in both worlds, fully belonging to neither.
- Third generation — essentially just American, experiencing this as a purely American crisis
  until their family's origin makes them a target of suspicion from their own neighbors.

Boston's Brighton neighborhood has historical Russian Jewish immigrant community presence —
provides authentic setting texture without invention.

The central dramatic question is not whether they support the invasion (most of them almost
certainly do not). It is what the invasion does to how their American neighbors look at them,
how they look at themselves, and whether any of them have family still in Russia who are
either complicit in the invasion or themselves victims of conscription into something they
didn't choose.

--- 3F: UKRAINIAN CHARACTERS ---

STATUS: Concept confirmed. Undeveloped. To be returned to during Game 1/2 campaign
development passes.

Ukrainian characters to be introduced — placement undecided between Game 1, Game 2, or both.
Primary placement instinct: Game 2, Ducky's campaign, where Ukrainian military and
intelligence cooperation with NATO is most plausible and historically grounded. Game 1 could
carry a lighter touch — brief appearance in a Eastern European mission context.

Function: Lens, not commentary. Ukraine is structurally present in the story already given
the geopolitical context of Russian expansionism. Ukrainian characters don't arrive as a
political statement — they arrive as people who have been living inside this reality longer
than anyone else in the story.

The Ukrainian innovation angle — drone warfare, electronic warfare, urban defense, distributed
resistance command — is directly relevant to existing gameplay and tactical themes and gives
Ukrainian characters specific professional competence that integrates naturally.

The polarization note: Ukrainian society contains real internal tensions. Treating Ukraine as
a monolithic heroic symbol would be as reductive as any other simplification. The lens
approach handles this. Handle with more care than other threads given the proximity of the
fictional world to ongoing real-world events. Return to develop during campaign detail passes.

--- 3G: UK SUBPLOT / BOSTON (LT. VICKERS) ---

STATUS: Previously flagged. Cross-referencing here for completeness.

Lt. Dorothy Anne Vickers (inspired in bearing by Lt. Lilly-Mae Fisher, fully fictionalized).
SAS, SBS, Royal Marines, potentially Royal Navy. British forces in occupied Boston — the
revolutionary history inversion (British in Boston) as deliberate thematic layer. Tea Party-

SECTION 4: SEA SPEAR EXPANSION
============================================================

STATUS: Concept confirmed. Structure locked. Timeline placement undecided.

Title: Sea Spear (confirmed — Sea Spire is retired)
Playable Unit: Nereid Black — Grim (primary), Rime, Undertow, Lantern
Setting: Baltic front (primary operational theater)
Relationship to Main Games: Expansion, separate from main trilogy campaigns

Structural Framework (Confirmed):
Sea Spear covers events the main campaigns don't show — gap-filling rather than retreading
known ground. Partial, significant overlaps with Ducky's campaign primarily, possibly Romeo's
depending on timeline placement. Those overlaps serve a specific purpose: giving the player
a new angle on events they already experienced, widening the geopolitical picture, and
potentially seeding retroactive intelligence pointing toward things the main campaigns only
partially revealed.

The retroactive intelligence seeding function is particularly valuable — Sea Spear could
make a second playthrough of the main games feel different because the player now recognizes
things they lacked context for the first time through. Kaliningrad harbor is already a
confirmed setting element from the Hull Breach mission, giving Sea Spear a natural geographic
anchor.

Timeline placement: UNDECIDED. Could sit before the main Game 2 events, run parallel to
them, or occupy a window between Games 2 and 3. To be determined when the main campaign
structures of Games 2 and 3 are more fully developed — the right window will likely become
obvious at that point.

SECTION 5: ISRAELI SUBPLOT ARCHITECTURE
============================================================

STATUS: Framework confirmed. Characters sketched. All undeveloped beyond framework level.
To be returned to for full development.

--- 5A: EXISTING CANON CONNECTIONS (from Game 1 notes) ---

The following are already established in the Game 1 campaign notes and are load-bearing
for the Israeli subplot:

BASTION INFOTECH: An Israeli defense-oriented software company confirmed as a front for
a Mossad and CIA black op run as part of the PANDORA plan. The shipment Spook's team
tracks in Game 1 is contracted in Bastion Infotech's name. This is the primary existing
Israeli institutional fingerprint on the PANDORA conspiracy.

ELIEZER BARUCH YADAT: Former Israeli PM, now working for Atlas Solutions (Singapore-based
global PMC, recently contracted to run operations security for multiple GenCorp sites
worldwide). Named in Game 1 collectible intel as a key connection to TETHERBREAK. Spook's
team infiltrates the Montoya Estate gala specifically to gather intel on connections between
Brodie van Horne, Reginald Stockbridge, and Yadat. He is already one of the most deeply
embedded figures in the entire conspiracy architecture — touching TETHERBREAK, the Shadow
Underground, GenCorp, and Atlas Solutions simultaneously.

YITZHAK BEN-TOV: Sitting Israeli PM in the game world. Netanyahu analogue. Already noted
in the canon.

PALISADE SYSTEMS: US defense contractor with confirmed ties to Israel. Listed recipient
of drone countermeasure technology from Bastion Infotech.

--- 5B: POST-PANDORA ISRAEL (CONFIRMED FRAMEWORK) ---

Israel partially survived the PANDORA infrastructure blackout because they had enough
foreknowledge — obtained through Yadat's Bastion Infotech connection and his pipeline to
Ben-Tov's government — to develop partial countermeasures before the attacks hit. Israel
pursues its own survival agenda exclusively post-PANDORA. No obligation to the US, which
is assessed as having handed itself to its enemies through conspiracy and institutional rot.
Previous military relationships and transactions do not change the calculus.

Chinese moves in the Middle East place Israeli interests in direct conflict with Chinese
strategic expansion — not ideologically, just geographically and materially. Israel doesn't
need to hate China. They just need China to stop operating in their backyard.

--- 5C: THE THREE ISRAELI FIGURES (CONFIRMED FRAMEWORK) ---

ELIEZER BARUCH YADAT — Former PM, Atlas Solutions. Antagonistic role in the conspiracy
architecture. Not a state actor — a private power player whose Israeli identity is part of
his history but whose current loyalties are to the network he operates within. The
conspiracy's Israeli face rather than Israel's face. His relationship to Bastion Infotech
and TETHERBREAK is load-bearing. Whether Ben-Tov knows the full extent of Yadat's
involvement or chooses not to examine it too closely is deliberately ambiguous.
ATLAS SOLUTIONS + GENCORP OVERLAP WITH WEI JUNLONG: Yadat's Atlas Solutions has GenCorp
security contracts. Wei Junlong operates through GenCorp as Jonathan Wei. These two figures
are therefore operating in the same layer. Whether they have had direct contact — and what
the nature of that contact is — is a significant potential Game 3 revelation.

YITZHAK BEN-TOV — Sitting Israeli PM. Legitimate state actor making calculated survival
decisions. Not entirely legitimate in practice — using the existential crisis as political
cover, forcing people to look the other way on due process and political integrity because
survival is a more urgent concern. Ben-Tov and Yadat are connected transactionally: Yadat
has been feeding Ben-Tov select intelligence (including enough about PANDORA to enable
partial countermeasures) through the Atlas/Bastion pipeline. Ben-Tov knows Yadat is a
source. How much he knows about the full extent of Yadat's network is the load-bearing
ambiguity of his character. He has a stake in whether the player succeeds or fails in
exposing Yadat. His placement in Game 2 or Game 3 is undecided.

ANONYMOUS ISRAELI INTELLIGENCE LIAISON — Distinct from both Yadat and Ben-Tov. A working
intelligence officer, probably younger generation, operating entirely off books. Passes
Chinese military intelligence to player-adjacent characters because it serves Israeli
interests for someone capable of hurting China to have it. Motivated purely by strategic
calculation, not sentiment or ideology. Institutional background: Mossad most likely,
Unit 8200 possible depending on specific function. Whether they act with quiet unofficial
sanction from Ben-Tov's government or are genuinely rogue is deliberately ambiguous.
Identity anonymous to the player initially — revealed gradually. Narrative entry point
is through the Bastion Infotech thread. The load-bearing moral tension of their arc is
the question of what they knew about what Bastion Infotech was actually used for.
Placement and timing undecided.

--- 5D: THE SLOW REVEAL (CONFIRMED STRUCTURE) ---

The player's accumulated understanding across Games 1-3 eventually reveals that Bastion
Infotech, Atlas Solutions, and GenCorp are all nodes in the same network. The Israeli
subplot isn't a separate thread grafted onto the main conspiracy — it was always inside it.
Bastion Infotech was in the Game 1 canon from the beginning. Atlas Solutions personnel
were at the Frankfurt warehouse. GenCorp runs through everything. The Israeli dimension
was embedded in the story from the start. The revelation in Game 3 is not that Israel is
connected. The revelation is that it was never not connected.

--- 6A: SEATTLE PORT MISSION (SPOOK CAMPAIGN) ---

STATUS: Early concept. Parked for development.

Spook is sent to the Port of Seattle to interdict a ship loaded with explosives before
it detonates in port — Beirut explosion scale and style as the tonal/destructive reference.
Perpetrating faction undecided (Russian, North Korean, Chinese, or proxy). Currently floating
without a narrative anchor in the Godkiller campaign structure. No confirmed crossover with
other player campaigns. The West Coast was the original amphibious invasion beachhead in
Game 1 — a Spook mission in Seattle carries the accumulated weight of what happened to
that coastline across the first two games. The mission's eventual narrative framing should
acknowledge this rather than ignore it.

--- 6B: WEST COAST THEATER (UNRESOLVED) ---

STATUS: Gap acknowledged. Resolution undecided.

California invasion partially visible in Game 1. Game 2 implies ongoing West Coast fighting
without detailed treatment. This gap is either material for a dedicated DLC covering the
West Coast conventional military resistance theater, or integration into a Game 3 campaign
(possibly Spook's given the Seattle concept, or a fourth campaign thread if Game 3 expands).
Deciding which of these it is will shape how much weight Seattle and the broader West Coast
carry in Game 3. Return to when Game 3 campaign structure is more fully mapped.
