Rank at exit:   Specialist (E-4)
Appears in:     Romeo's Game 2 campaign / American Freedom
Location:       Calloway area, Kentucky

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CHARACTER FOUNDATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pat is a trans man and former enlisted U.S. Army 68W combat medic who completed
one enlistment and returned to the Calloway area as a civilian before the
occupation. He is calm, dry, precise under pressure, and indispensable to the
early resistance cell because he gives it something amateur resistance groups
usually lack: credible casualty stabilization and practical sustainment.

His transness matters to his history of institutional betrayal under tightening
Crodyn-era policy, but it is not his only or primary scene-to-scene function.
He should read first as a capable adult with useful hands, not as a thesis
statement. The betrayal axis is institutional/state/military rather than
familial; his parents remain supportive, and Renata Voss's support should be
shown through trust and natural respect rather than speeches.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LOADOUT SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Primary:
  Colt LE6920 16.1" 5.56 AR-pattern carbine with Aimpoint PRO, Streamlight
  ProTac Rail Mount 2, Blue Force Gear Vickers two-point sling, and Magpul
  MOE furniture. No laser. No suppressor. Kept light to free Pat for rapid
  casualty treatment.

Sidearm:
  Glock 19 Gen5 with Streamlight TLR-7A in Safariland ALS holster.

Medical / utility:
  Leatherman Raptor Rescue trauma shears
  Benchmade Triage rescue-oriented folding knife
  Reorganized surplus aid bag with tourniquets, gauze, chest seals, airway kit,
  decompression needles, gloves, tape, casualty markers, and Sharpie.

Gear profile:
  Cut-down surplus TAP rig, med pouches displacing ammunition, civilian work
  jacket or muted softshell over low-profile armor when available, Mechanix
  gloves, waterproof notepad, durable hiking/work boots.

Design rule:
  Practical former enlisted medic, not pseudo-operator. Does not name weapons.
  Rifle exists to create space and buy treatment time.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SQUAD FUNCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pat is the early resistance cell's casualty stabilizer, field medic, and quiet
adult counterweight to younger, more impulsive energy — especially Cody
Beaumont. Cody moves forward on nerve and impulse. Pat is the reason the cell
has a chance of surviving what that impulse costs.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VOICE / USE GUIDANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pat should be calm, dry, exact, and pressure-flattening. He does not dramatize
wounds or fill silence with comfort-performance. He says what matters:

  "Tourniquet high."
  "Hold pressure there."
  "Need thirty seconds."
  "Keep talking to me."

He is competent rather than theatrical, useful rather than flashy, and should
strengthen casualty stakes, improvised treatment scenes, and the credibility of
Romeo's early Kentucky resistance cell.
