SECTION 2 — THREAD INTENT AND METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

The timeline was built to serve as a structural skeleton for the entire trilogy,
anchoring historical decisions and ensuring synchronicity of events across all
three games and all DLC content. The goal is to prevent continuity fractures as
Game 3 development deepens.

Methodology:
- Fixed canonical anchors established first (September 3 PANDORA attacks,
  November 3 election, Cold Frontier 07, etc.)
- Uncertain dates flagged with [~] to indicate they need formal locking
- Events confirmed across multiple project files are treated as canonical
- Gaps and structural unknowns are explicitly flagged at the end
- Game 2 treated as the more developed entry and built out in greater detail
  than Game 1, which is documented at the campaign-arc level

DEEP BACKGROUND (PRE-GAME)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[~2006-2007] — COLD FRONTIER 07

Finnish special forces winter exercise in Lapland. Russian Vympel team executes
Operation Parhelion-53: a false flag designed to frame Finnish troops as having
attacked Russian border forces on Russian soil.

Frost kills Vympel operators during the incident. Takes a 9mm round to the face
point-blank. Falls 96 feet into an icy lake, drowns his attacker underwater,
pulls himself ashore, loses consciousness. Comrade Jussi Mustonen is killed
during the operation.

Unmarked Russian operator bodies surface — potential international incident.
Averted by a quiet Finland-NATO-Russia trilateral back-channel agreement. All
reports are buried. Parhelion-53 fails in its objective. Frost's permanent
facial scarring dates to this event.

The FSB officer who signed off on Parhelion-53: a then-middling operations
officer named Mikhail Kostropov. By the late 2020s, Kostropov has risen to
become Baranov's chief of intelligence staff and head of the FSB.

[~2007 ONWARD] — GLUSHKIN DEEP COVER BEGINS

Glushkin adopts the ghost identity of Brent Henry Tibbets — a homeless,
disappeared 24-year-old from Haverhill, MA — under FSB financing. Enrolls at
MIT. Completes dual electrical and aeronautics engineering degrees by
approximately 2011. Takes a position at Specter Aerospace, Peabody, MA.
Deep cover becomes operational.

[~2010s] — STRUCTURAL BUILDUP DECADE

Montoya Farm underground complex becomes operational under Brodie van Horne,
later transferred into Goldberg's orbit. Runs MKULTRA-adjacent conditioning
programs, global trafficking infrastructure, and the Shadow Underground's
blackmail architecture.

TETHERBREAK is developed as a CIA black program. Nominally framed as a
penetration test against US autonomous weapons command-and-control
infrastructure. True purpose: an insurance policy for the global elite,
ensuring autonomous weapons systems can never be turned against them.

PANDORA is conceived as the operational weaponization of TETHERBREAK against
US soil. Originally a contingency. Later activated.

GenCorp grows into a global surveillance data and defense ICT giant. Berkhan,
Crodyn, and Ruetger accumulate major shareholdings.

Kostropov rises through FSB ranks.

[~EARLY TO MID 2020s]

Crodyn serves first and second presidential terms. Systematically dismantles
FEMA and federal emergency response capability through cronyism, insider dealing,
and faux-reformist policy. When the attacks come, there is not enough
infrastructure left to help even a fraction of those in need.

Compromised SCOTUS justices Howard and Pedilla quietly develop legal rationale
for suspending the two-term presidential limit under wartime emergency
conditions. Held in reserve, awaiting use.

KINGMAKER network reaches peak operational maturity. Goldberg is its center of
gravity.

La Avispa (Yelena Volkova, formerly of FSB) defects following a botched
surveillance operation. Surfaces eventually as a top-ranking enforcer within
Los Sietes.

SPOOK'S CAMPAIGN — RIPTIDE RED
[~Spring 2027 – September 3, 2028]
========================================

[~SPRING 2027]

Victor Two Bravo (Spook, Tripper, Smoke) dispatched by handler Regent (Mullen)
to Amsterdam Schiphol. Arrive undercover as Canadian overland travelers and
amateur filmmakers. Meet Emperor (Cameron Steele) at Eastern Docklands
warehouse. Briefed on PANDORA and a suspicious Turkmenistan tech shipment in
transit to the US via Vienna. Issued mission equipment and mobile ops RV.

Team travel to Vienna. Receive brief from Archduke (Kathy Hughes) at the US
Embassy. Safehouse raid in Hernals (Vienna, 17th district) — apartment initially
empty, then compromised by a returning woman and armed men. Rooftop chase across
Hernals, onto a train platform, into a pedestrian underpass. Motorcycle
exfiltration. High-speed pursuit on Vienna beltway — van driver killed, van
destroyed. Bikes ditched at Port of Vienna. Tablet recovered from safehouse.

[~LATE SPRING 2027]

Tablet Intel leads to Betta Logistics / Simenon Navarre, near Frankfurt Airport.
Mission: Above My Pay Grade — team infiltrate Navarre's house. Navarre gives up
Warehouse 46, Frankfurt Airport.
Optional: Navarre and Budanova given new identities (relocated to Evora,
Alentejo, Portugal — Navarre becomes Simon-Pierre Aubert, Swiss French,
semi-retired freight consultant; Budanova becomes Katja Reimann, Austrian).

Team assault Warehouse 46. Atlas personnel present. Shipment already moved
following an apparent tip-off — source unknown at this stage. Warehouse
destroyed. Hard drive recovered by Tripper.

Tripper partially decrypts hard drive: fragments of PANDORA and TETHERBREAK.
Jaeger (Stanzler) at DAOG/PYTHIA matches cryptic flight codes to privately
chartered cargo flights, all declared as restricted drone countermeasure hardware
for US defense contractors.

[~SUMMER 2027]

Intel trail leads to GenCorp regional HQ, Abu Dhabi, UAE.
Mission: Infrastructure Day — team rappel into Abu Dhabi skyscraper data center.
Shipping records recovered. Easter egg: Atlas recruitment poster reading "Feel
like a hero?" has been vandalized in the bathroom to read "Do you feel like a
hero yet?" — Spec Ops: The Line reference. Poster seeded in Game 1 and Game 2.
Trail leads to Agarwal Berkhan (codename: Baloo).

Team dispatched to Medan City, Indonesia.
Operation: DRAGONSLAYER / Mission: Man From Medan.
Assault Berkhan's Cemara Asri compound. Berkhan escapes by helicopter.
Helicopter shot down mid-flight by a "malfunctioning" Indonesian Skynex SAM —
actually TETHERBREAK sabotage, not recognized by the team at the time.
Berkhan killed. Death officially blamed on Indonesian Islamic terrorists.

Macmillan (Bishop) contacts team: Regent has been "urgently reassigned."
Victor Two Bravo now reports directly to Bishop.
New orders: Miami, FL. Team fly Frankfurt to Orlando. Drive to Doral safehouse.

[~JULY-AUGUST 2028]

Rendezvous with NSA contacts Simmons and Lynch in Medley, FL.
Intel: restricted components tracked from Miami-Dade Airport to the Kali Yuga
Surf Club (KYSC), who have moved containers into the Desautels Resort Hotel,
Miami Beach.

Mission: Pandora's Box — team infiltrate Desautels Resort Hotel.
Discovery: KYSC members are Montoya Farm conditioning subjects bearing
CogniConnect neural implants. Team defeats KYSC. Disable one PANDORA device
(one cell — Miami averted as a primary target). Los Sietes cartel security
overruns the hotel. Cartel helicopter engages. Fertilizer bomb detonates at
mezzanine lobby — team escape through elevator shaft into parking garage.
Hotel largely destroyed in the firefight and explosion. Team exfiltrate.

KYSC members bear Montoya Farm symbol tattoos — lead to Montoya Farm.

[~AUGUST 2028]

Team acquire cover via ambush of Uncle Jack Pruitt's biker gang contacts.
Infiltrate Montoya Farm compound, New Mexico.
Mission: Burning Daylight — infiltration takes place during a gala event.
Stockbridge is present at the gala.
Spook tails Stockbridge with directional mic. Recovers keycard from ballroom
annex cloakroom desk. Accesses security room. Discovers CCTV feeds showing a
vast, unknown underground complex beneath the farm.

Team infiltrate underground via concealed basement hatch near pool machinery
room. Interior: makeshift prison, inpatient medical facility with operating
theater, snuff production sets, conditioning rooms (the "Ludovico Room"),
dormitories with fake windows, underground plaza with simulated sky. Montoya
Farm symbols on the walls. Tripper plants hardware implant in server room.
Extracts encrypted archive. Team exfiltrate via hidden vertical shaft behind
a filing cabinet.

Tripper decrypts archive. Contents revealed:
- KINGMAKER architecture in full
- Global intelligence agency participation documented
- KYSC/PANDORA/TETHERBREAK connections
- Riptide Red conspiracy structure
- The suppressed Riptide Red analyst report (the document itself was purged)
- Crodyn's criminal and foreign intelligence ties
- DARMA (NSA program suppressing domestic inquiry into the Shadow Underground)

Team decide to move directly on Goldberg. KINGMAKER plan activated. Travel to
US Virgin Islands.

[~LATE AUGUST 2028]

Mission: Come Fly With Me — St. Croix, US Virgin Islands.
Team stake out a fishing compound. Eliminate 7-man security detail. Coerce
foreman to refuel a Found BFA-2 floatplane. Smoke flies the team dark toward
East McFarlane Island — engine off, landing in a small cove.

Mission: Sins of Our Fathers — East McFarlane Island.
Team infiltrate Goldberg's private island complex. Discover evidence of
trafficking operations, illegal material production, and ritual crimes
in the underground complex.
War Wolves dispatched by Stockbridge to intercept: Crimson One, Crimson Two,
Crimson Three. Team defeats all three. CogniConnect implants detonate on death,
destroying their faces. Identities never confirmed.
Team corner Goldberg and Stockbridge at the helipad.
Macmillan (Bishop) contacts Spook — orders him to stand down and give Goldberg
safe passage. Victor Two Bravo declared rogue.

SEPTEMBER 3, 2028 — THE PANDORA ATTACKS [CANONICAL ANCHOR]

Nationwide PANDORA activation. Autonomous air and ground drone systems hijacked
across multiple US cities via TETHERBREAK — turned against strategic military
assets and civilian populations. Publicly attributed by the Crodyn administration
to China.

US cyber defense and EW countermeasures blacked out.

Simultaneously:
- Russia opens the Baltic front in Western Europe
- West Coast amphibious invasion begins (Russian, North Korean, Iranian coalition)
- China invades Taiwan, exploiting the US cyber blackout — annexation begins

Crodyn declares wartime national emergency. Howard and Pedilla's SCOTUS
rationale invoked. Two-term presidential limit suspended. Crodyn positioned
for an unconstitutional third term.

ROMEO'S CAMPAIGN — CRITTER SQUAD
[~Early 2028 – September 2028]
========================================

[~EARLY TO MID 2028]

Fire Team Delta (Romeo, Dingo, Howler, Beaver) deployed to eastern Poland to
shore up NATO's eastern border against the anticipated Russian invasion.

Russian forces cross. Delta encircled near the southern Polish border. Escape
encirclement. Evade drones. Move through eastern Czech Republic and Austria to

Delta and AEGIS Squad jointly protect classified geospatial intelligence
infrastructure across Europe:
- Svalbard (AEGIS primary datacenter)
- French Alps
- Lemnos, Aegean
- Hindeloopen, Netherlands

Command orders Delta back to the continental United States. All flights grounded.
AEGIS assists Delta across Brittany to Ile Longue French Naval Base.
Team intercept enemy sabotage operation targeting the garrison and nuclear
submarines Le Terrible and Le Vigilant. Garrison secured.

AEGIS diverted: Maasvlakte Oil and Gas Terminal, Rotterdam — combat alongside
Dutch Royal Marines against Russian special forces.

Delta rendezvous with USS District of Columbia (nuclear submersible). Transit
to US West Coast. Surface off Ventura, California.

[~AUGUST TO SEPTEMBER 2028]

Delta joins US Army infantry moving toward Port of Los Angeles.
Clear DPRK forces from North Hollywood (mission: Down Rodeo) and South
Beverly Hills. Reach Port of Los Angeles / Long Beach — secured by USMC platoon.

Battle of Signal Hill — defend Long Beach Airport against DPRK assault.
Attackers pushed back. Outcome of André vs. Romeo (Rangers push vs. Marines
consolidate) — TBD. Coronado friction/resolution to mirror Signal Hill structure.

Convoy south along highway toward San Diego.
Distress signal received from Naval Air Station North Island, Coronado —
large amphibious attack incoming.

Battle of Coronado Island — Delta fights alongside US Army and Marines to repel
joint Russian/DPRK/Iranian amphibious assault on the Pacific Navy Carrier Strike
Group. [CAMPAIGN 1 FINALE]

INTERWAR PERIOD — AUTUMN 2028 TO EARLY 2029
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SEPTEMBER TO OCTOBER 2028]

PANDORA aftermath: mass civilian casualties. Infrastructure collapse across
multiple US cities. FEMA non-functional due to Crodyn's systematic dismantling.

UNHMNA (United Nations Humanitarian Mission to North America) deployed.
White UN vehicles and foreign-uniformed troops visible across American cities
and suburbs. UN blue-helmeted soldiers at checkpoints in familiar American
landscapes — a deeply jarring visual juxtaposition, recalling imagery historically
associated with conflict zones in Africa, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia
now transposed onto American soil.

SPRINGBLOAT (NSA) activated — suppresses domestic communications and inquiry
about the true nature of the attacks.

Russian forces continue pressure on European NATO members. Baltic front active.

Chinese annexation of Taiwan completed. China monopolizes TSMC foundry
capabilities. Emerges as world's premier digital and military superpower.
ASML supply disruption affects autonomous systems rebuild globally — including
the AEGIS program. (Flagged: potential dramatic irony for Ducky's Game 2
campaign — Ducky may be operating inside this consequence without full awareness
of the connection.)

NOVEMBER 3, 2028 — US PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION [CANONICAL ANCHOR]

Election suspended or invalidated under Crodyn's wartime emergency declaration.
Howard and Pedilla SCOTUS rationale upheld. Crodyn remains in office for an
unconstitutional third term.

[~LATE 2028]

Goldberg surrenders to US authorities. Charged with mishandling sensitive
documents related to the Desautels/KYSC bombing — a deliberately reduced charge.
Plea deal arranged with Florida prosecutor Lawrence Kaylor. Sentenced to 24
months at Coleman Federal Correctional Facility, Sumterville, FL. Parole
eligibility at 6 months.

[~EARLY 2029] — SUMTERVILLE PRISON OPERATION

Los Sietes dual-team assault on Coleman Federal Correctional Facility.

Team One: assault on the general population wing. El Furtivo (Pablo Ignacio
"El Furtivo" Hernandez Diaz — high-ranking Los Sietes-affiliated human
trafficker and Goldberg business associate) extracted in the chaos.

Team Two (operating via paid-off correctional staff): Goldberg has been secured
in the medical wing under the pretext of a routine medical checkup. Team plants
a mutilated, DNA-matched corpse in a destroyed cell block — the DNA matches
Goldberg's, courtesy of cartel prison contacts. Goldberg is loaded into an
oblong box (coffin-like), the lid sealed, and the box is loaded into a waiting
hearse positioned inside the facility hours before the assault. COs drive the
hearse out the back of the medical wing. Cartel men follow in a black SUV.
The chaos of the general wing assault provides cover. Goldberg is extracted
successfully.

The world believes Goldberg is dead. He goes underground. Begins rebuilding
resources and networks. Launches an intelligence counter-offensive against
Crodyn and his circle.

ROMEO'S CAMPAIGN — "GOOD MORNING, AMERICA"
========================================

Setting: Russian-occupied Kentucky. Fictional town of Calloway and nearby
community Redbud.

Opening cinematic: Jesse Welles' "Good Morning, America" plays over a dawn
aerial shot of Calloway's main street. Russian flags. Troops at roadblocks.
Burnt-out houses. Survivors picking through rubble. Foreign occupation rendered
in a completely familiar American landscape. Music cue function: productive
dissonance — the warmth of the song against the horror of the image.
Established design principle: "Good Morning, America" against occupied Kentucky
is the model for music-to-scene relationship across all Project Warriors trailers
and campaign openings.

Romeo makes contact with the resistance cell:
- Dale Pruitt
- Renata Voss
- Cody Beaumont
- Patrick "Pat" Napier
- Frank Kowalski

Three-movement campaign arc: Roots / Pressure / Breaking Point

Missions (confirmed): Low Ground, Dead Air, The Redbud Vehicles, Farm Road,
Second Level

In Low Ground: Player observes Glushkin (operating as Brent Tibbets, his
deep cover identity) conducting an interview with resistance-adjacent civilian
Gene Marlow. This is one of the player's first implicit encounters with the
deeper conspiracy in the Game 2 setting.

Cody Beaumont arc: develops over the campaign, culminates in his death during
a detainee rescue operation. Loading screen following the mission: image of a
young man suspended from a highway gantry, a cardboard sign accusing him of
treason against Russia. Never explicitly confirmed as Cody. Strategically
ambiguous — left for the player to carry.

Later: Romeo reunites with Dingo, Howler, and Beaver.

American Freedom Corps (AFC) founded: the Calloway resistance cell becomes the
seed of a broader national movement. Romeo becomes its de facto first figurehead.

Late-campaign decision point: opportunity to kill Glushkin. Outcome TBD.

Flagged: DON'T TREAD militia recognition beat (Act Two, Romeo) — to be developed
in the narrative thread.

Note on AC-130 scene: The AC-130 "Angel" scene is a confirmed set-piece in
Romeo's Game 2 campaign (American Freedom). Full treatment exists in master
scenes file. Protected line: "American Freedom, over." The AC-130 sequence
has been developed in full in the aircraft scenes thread and master file.

Red Dawn paratrooper drop over the Smoky Mountains: confirmed Romeo Game 2
scene. Concept-stage only. Also flagged as a standalone trailer asset.

SPOOK'S CAMPAIGN — GODKILLER
========================================

Three operational tracks running simultaneously:
- Protecting Budanova
- Intelligence manipulation (Tripper's track)
- Covert resistance support (Smoke's track)

Regent reunion: Mullen resurfaces. His "urgent reassignment" from Game 1 must
be accounted for — was he burned, turned, or protected? This needs a timeline
entry once developed.

Central narrative question: "How do you kill a man who's already dead?"
Spook hunting Goldberg — a man the world believes is dead, beyond conventional
reach, operating outside all systems.

La Avispa (Yelena Volkova): confirmed to appear in Godkiller. Several missions
force Spook and La Avispa to work together despite initial mutual suspicion.
Dramatic circumstances give them no option but to depend on each other. A quiet
trust and an unusual bond develops. Spook learns her private story over the
course of their ordeal — her youth near the Black Sea coast in southern Russia,
her promising career in Russian intelligence, the botched surveillance operation
that got her burned by her own team and superiors, her defection, life on the
run through Venezuela, Cuba, Panama, and eventually Mexico, her rise within
Los Sietes. She learns a fragment of Spook's story in return. A moment of
genuine connection — possibly something close to fleeting romantic tension —
but the story forces them apart before it can become anything defined. Left open
for a possible future reunion.

Goldberg confrontation scene: confirmed late-game dramatic asset. Goldberg as
the natural voice for articulating "the false flag consumed by a bigger
conspiracy" — a significant dramatic asset, protect placement. The scene should
not arrive too early and must be earned.

PANDORA ending: Option B or close to C. Remediation underway but incomplete at
the end of Game 3. Not a clean resolution. Do not resolve prematurely.

   Game 2 introduces a Chinese military operation targeting northeastern

   campaign events. Home Defense DLC is the relevant content container.

6. AFC GROWTH ARC
   Romeo's campaign seeds the American Freedom Corps in Calloway. When and how
   does it become a national movement? What is its state by Game 3? Relevant
   to Romeo's Game 3 arc. Dale Pruitt's potential New America accelerationist

7. FIND REGENT CROSS-GAME HIDDEN QUEST
   Dead drop cache on East McFarlane Island. Symbol seeded at Montoya Farm
   (tunnels, Game 1) and on the island. Symbol appears again in a Game 2
   environment and unlocks a storage unit or safe deposit box containing
   Regent's unredacted Riptide Red report. Cache must be placed in the timeline
   before Game 2 events begin. Who placed it and when?

8. PANDORA KILL SWITCH / WEI JUNLONG FRACTURE LINE
   The Taiwanese semiconductor engineer connected to Wei Junlong's personal
   fracture is the likely architect of the TETHERBREAK kill switch. Their
   foundry/firmware knowledge makes them the person who can reverse-engineer
   PANDORA's persistence vulnerability. Wei's fracture and the technical
   resolution of PANDORA may be the same thread. PROTECTED — do not resolve
   prematurely.

9. CORYS SQUAD DEVELOPMENT
   Valkyrie and Brick promo clips cannot be produced until CORYS squad is
   developed. This is a blocking dependency.
