## Project-memory / project-facing lock summary

### Brno mission viability
- **Brno is a valid and strong mission location**, best used in **Ducky Game 2** rather than Romeo.
- The mission should not use postcard/downtown Brno framing.
- The strongest Brno identity is **urban infrastructure / expo-service / freight-adjacent / industrial civic connective tissue** rather than tourism or skyline iconography.

### Mission lock
- **Working title:** OPEN CIRCUIT
- **Campaign placement:** Ducky Game 2, mid-campaign
- **Primary location:** Brno Exhibition Centre / Výstaviště district, especially the **eastern / south-eastern Velodrom-side service edge**
- **Mission identity:** urban infrastructure interdiction / technical recovery / emergency exfil
- **Narrative purpose:** prove that the enemy’s technical/logistics layer is embedded inside ordinary European civilian infrastructure

### Geometry / location rule
- The mission is built around **service roads, loading access, utility corridors, sealed halls, contractor access, back-of-house expo architecture, and a compressed urban choke point**
- Airport and wider freight-yard material remain valid **secondary or future-use spaces**, not the primary identity of OPEN CIRCUIT

### SHIELD integration lock
- SHIELD should be present as an **attached recovery / exploitation element**, not as co-equal protagonist ownership
- Best structural use:
  - AEGIS confirms and secures the site
  - SHIELD exploits, scans, seizes, and triages the relay floor
  - lockdown fractures both elements
  - SHIELD is pinned on a parallel route
  - AEGIS is pinned trying to reach the exfil corridor
  - KERAUNOS breaks the choke and enables recombination/exfil

### KERAUNOS integration lock
- **KERAUNOS is the correct epic-scene integration for OPEN CIRCUIT**
- Exact use: the strike hits the **Velodrom-facing eastern / south-eastern service-road choke** after site lockdown and the AEGIS/SHIELD split
- The strike is a **surgical urban pressure-break**, not area devastation

### KERAUNOS visibility refinement
- The Brno version keeps the strike **ground-observed, fast, and brief**
- Adjustment locked from later discussion:
  - the Rafales should be **briefly clearer and more legible than mere blurred silhouettes**
  - still not treated as glamour shots
  - readable enough to catch delta-canard form, loaded wings, pod/store presence, and French identity in flashes
  - still subordinate to the trapped street and strike geometry
- Working rule:
  - **terrifyingly legible for a moment**
  - not air-show framing, not hero-jet composition

### Ducky tone / use rule
- Ducky remains the emotional and playable center
- He should stay compressed, analytical, quiet under pressure
- The mission should never make him speechy or admiring of the strike
- KERAUNOS is necessary, not theatrical
