MISSION IDENTITY
OPEN CIRCUIT is an urban infrastructure interdiction, technical recovery, and emergency exfil mission set in the back-of-house Brno Exhibition Centre / Výstaviště district.
It is not a downtown sightseeing battle, Prague-lite city-center mission, or generic airport raid.

NARRATIVE FUNCTION
The mission proves that the hostile European technical layer is embedded inside ordinary civilian systems: trade-fair logistics, contractor access, cargo handling, temporary installs, protected commercial movement, power, cooling, and service routes.
Core thesis:
The enemy is not just moving through Europe. They are hiding inside how Europe already works.

MISSION FLOW
1. COLD APPROACH
Enter during partial civic normality. Closed tram stops, parked contractor vehicles, intermittent work lights, and a dormant-looking district.
Confirm cameras, power, movement, and candidate halls without broad compromise.

2. FALSE SITE / REAL PATTERN
The obvious floor contains staged technical clutter.
Ducky identifies that the service route is protected more heavily than the visible hardware.
The mission pivots from the decoy to the east-side service artery.

3. SERVICE-SIDE INGRESS
Move through loading access, utility alleys, freight doors, plant rooms, cable routes, and temporary walling.
Combat begins as short, selective contact against deniable contractor-cover security.

4. LIVE RELAY FLOOR
Breach a repurposed hall/conference interface containing portable racks, cooling, hardened power management, fiber, cased components, workstations, and wipe preparation.
AEGIS secures the perimeter.
SHIELD enters, exploits, and removes the routing core.
Reveal: Brno is one node in a larger continental chain.

5. HARD LOCKDOWN / SPLIT
Shutters drop, section lights fail, vehicle barriers rise, and deniable security becomes overt military reaction.
SHIELD takes a parallel lower route with the package.
AEGIS is forced onto a mirrored eastward route.
The building, not melodrama, causes the split.

6. VELODROM-SIDE CHOKE / KERAUNOS
Both elements are pinned on parallel lines at the east/south-east service-road choke.
Enemy trucks, barriers, and elevated firing positions seal the only viable corridor.
The player confirms friendly geometry and designates the roadblock.

7. STRIKE / RECOMBINATION / EXFIL
KERAUNOS breaks the roadblock and strips overwatch.
AEGIS moves immediately through the breach.
SHIELD emerges with the package and any wounded.
Both elements recombine and escape through the short corridor before the second ring closes.
