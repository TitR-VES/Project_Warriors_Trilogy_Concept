1. UNIT ROLE / CANON POSITION
==================================================

SHIELD is now defined as DAOG’s hard-entry / sensitive-site exploitation / emergency recovery and denial squad, distinct from both AEGIS and CORYS.

Functional distinction:
- AEGIS = broader frontline special-operations strike / recon team
- CORYS = protection of command personnel and fixed critical assets
- SHIELD = breach, recover, stabilize, exploit, exfil; deny the asset if recovery fails

Use SHIELD when the objective is inside a collapsing, compromised, or time-sensitive interior site and must be recovered before sanitization, overrun, or internal destruction.
