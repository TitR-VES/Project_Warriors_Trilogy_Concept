03. ACT IV OPENER REFINEMENT — AID AND COMFORT
================================================================================

Mission title:
AID AND COMFORT.

Placement:
Act IV opener, after BLACKOUT MEDICINE.

Mission function:
Chris helps secure a relief convoy carrying limited generator fuel, medical supplies, batteries, and support gear for Rivergate / nearby public aid staging.

Attack / threat function:
The convoy is targeted, revealing to Chris and the player that FFSM / Jacobs & Reed hardliners are not only disrupting logistics. They are marking public aid itself as the next target.

Targeting logic:
Hardliners treat relief as collaboration, federal control, or betrayal. The public-aid apparatus becomes an ideological target because it demonstrates that civic survival can still exist outside their control.

Reveal methods:
The mission can make the targeting logic visible through one or more of:
- ambush / probing attack
- observers marking the convoy
- recovered notes or comms
- surviving attacker behavior
- evidence of pre-selected public-aid infrastructure targets

End-state purpose:
Set up the following mission where Chris tracks the actors preparing to strike the newly arrived aid infrastructure.

Tone rule:
AID AND COMFORT should not become generic convoy defense. Its main value is showing that relief itself has become contested authority.
