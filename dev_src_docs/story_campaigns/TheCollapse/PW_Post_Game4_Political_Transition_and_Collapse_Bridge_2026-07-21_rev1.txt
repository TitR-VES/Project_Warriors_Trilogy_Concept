PURPOSE
Define the political and institutional bridge from Game 4's ending into The
Collapse without turning the successor administration into a hidden villain or
inventing exact calendar dates.

PHASE I — THE RECKONING
- After Sunny's capture and the outcome of Spook's Game 4 campaign, the Hecatite
  network moves against Freedom and attempts to silence opposition.
- Gillian Stockbridge recovers sufficiently to stand trial.
- The Crodyn administration falls.
- Impeachment/trial and broader investigations reach corrupt officials and
  ideological appointees.
- Institutional purges are necessary but deepen public distrust and reduce
  administrative capacity.

PHASE II — RESTORATION ELECTION
- A legitimate election still occurs despite insurgent violence, propaganda,
  disrupted regions, and severe instability.
- The new administration has legal authority but inherits an impossible position.
- It is inexperienced and partially compromised by surviving influence networks.
- It is not secretly villainous; it is overwhelmed.

PHASE III — LONG UNRAVELING
- Federal practical control becomes increasingly uneven by region.
- NAF and other armed movements expand through governance, protection, logistics,
  aid, local influence, coercion, and violence rather than only open conquest.
- Recovery cannot keep pace with destruction.

PHASE IV — KINGMAKER AFTERMATH
- Goldberg may be defeated, but the wider kingmaker ecosystem persists in
  fragmented form through dark money, shell organizations, compromised actors,
  and surviving influence channels.

PHASE V — CONTINUED HUMANITARIAN MISSION
- International relief remains necessary because domestic reconstruction cannot
  meet nationwide need.
- America survives, but restoration remains incomplete.

PHASE VI — THE COLLAPSE
- The cumulative effects of PANDORA, World War III, corruption, economic
  disruption, insurgency, and institutional decay create the DLC's conditions.
- The Collapse campaign still occurs during the months before federal authority
  finally fails.

TIMEFRAME
The newer source refines the Game 4-to-Collapse bridge to approximately two to
three years. Treat this as a broad relative window, not an exact date.

NAME GOVERNANCE
Gillian Stockbridge is the repository-canonical name. Do not create a separate
“Justine Stockbridge” identity from conversational transcription.
