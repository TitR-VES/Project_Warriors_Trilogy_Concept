MISSION CONTEXT
Campaign: Spook Game 1 — RIPTIDE RED
Mission: Infrastructure Day
Location: GenCorp skyscraper, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
Playable character: Trevor “Spook” Gartner
Optional target: Leandro Petrus Blommaert

FUNCTION
This page replaces the older reading of Blommaert as a data-security executive with
the controlling physical-security / protective-operations role and defines the earned
exterior-rappel target-of-opportunity sequence.

Blommaert is not a mandatory boss. The player must create and recognize the opportunity.
His death or survival is a branch outcome with later continuity consequences.

OPTIONAL-KILL SETUP
Preferred scene:
- During the exterior-rappel phase, Blommaert steps onto a private exterior service
  balcony or recessed maintenance terrace outside the executive security suite.
- He is taking a secure phone call because internal rooms and channels may be compromised.
- Spook reaches the correct exterior position, inverts on the rappel line, and can take
  one clean suppressed shot from outside the tower.
- The beat may evoke an inverted tactical-rappel engagement, but must remain grounded,
  restrained, and short.

EARN CONDITIONS
The opportunity requires all or most of the following mission work:
1. Recover Blommaert's movement schedule, live camera feed, or security routing clue.
2. Disable, loop, or bypass the exterior camera covering the balcony/terrace.
3. Reach the correct rappel position before full-alarm or data-center escalation.
4. Wait for the narrow movement window instead of rushing.
5. Take a single clean suppressed shot.

PROMPT
ELIMINATE

“Take the shot” is an acceptable discarded alternative, but ELIMINATE is the controlling
prompt because it matches Spook's clinical operational vocabulary.

FAILURE / SURVIVAL STATES
Blommaert survives if:
- the player arrives too late;
- the player alarms the building early;
- exterior shutters deploy;
- the player misses;
- the player wounds but does not kill him;
- the player bypasses the opportunity;
- the player deliberately leaves him alive.

A surviving Blommaert learns from the encounter. Later security designed by him may
specifically counter vertical access, camera-loop expectations, false-route logic,
and clean balcony approaches.

MISSION CONSEQUENCES
If Blommaert is eliminated:
- GenCorp loses a high-value physical-security coordinator;
- the immediate response/exfil route may become cleaner or less adaptive;
- the player may recover optional intelligence from his security lane;
- JUDGE ME NOT unlocks if achievements are implemented.

If he survives:
- the mission remains completable;
- response pressure may remain higher;
- Blommaert is available for future Spook-campaign continuity.

No branch is globally canonical unless a later owner decision explicitly selects it.

ICON LOCK

Required visual elements:
- one single upside-down silhouette of Blommaert;
- back turned;
- holding a phone to his ear;
- viewed from Spook's inverted rappel perspective;
- the reticle center dot aligned on Blommaert's head, not the center of his back;
- an optional faint balcony rail / ledge that follows the same inverted perspective;
- no duplicate right-side-up silhouette;

Readability requirement:

legible at small achievement-list size.
