MISSION PURPOSE
First neighborhood-face test after HINGE.
The resistance clears and holds a visible run of storefronts, upper apartments, side
courtyard, and civic corner long enough for the block to face outward again.

THEMATIC FUNCTION
- hidden routes become visible civic ground;
- visibility itself becomes political and dangerous;
- civilians use the short way instead of the scared way;
- the neighborhood begins to re-inhabit its public surface.

BRIEFING CORE
The occupiers use the frontage as a pressure shelf with:
- corner hardpoint;
- two upper positions;
- courtyard feed;
- street response loop.

front of the block.”

long enough for the face to turn back outward.”

ELENA: “Long enough to matter. Short enough we can still survive it.”
ELENA: “You’re not taking a building today. You’re changing what a block looks like
when people risk looking at it.”
ELENA: “Open the face.”
