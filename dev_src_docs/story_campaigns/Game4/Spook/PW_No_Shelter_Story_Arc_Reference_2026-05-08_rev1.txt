PURPOSE
This page defines NO SHELTER, the preferred current mid-campaign / international-pursuit story arc for Spook’s Game 4 campaign Permanent Record.

CORE IDENTITY
NO SHELTER is the arc where the campaign proves that surviving networks, cover identities, elite social shells, and maritime / administrative escape infrastructure still believe they can turn movement into erasure. The arc’s job is to prove they cannot do so cleanly.

CURRENT CHAIN
1. Montreal bridge continuation into the European lead
2. Paris trailwork around Geoffrey-Guy Moffat and dead-network residue
3. ET IN ARCADIA EGO — Monaco / Marietta infiltration
4. RECEIVERSHIP — Haifa / Keter Maritime identity-confirmation mission

01. MONTREAL BRIDGE CONTINUATION
Montreal hands the campaign off from LINE OF FIRE into the international pursuit. Its job inside NO SHELTER is to preserve continuity, not to act like a fresh campaign start.

02. PARIS TRAILWORK
Paris remains load-bearing. It should preserve:
- the Geoffrey-Guy Moffat continuity seam
- the sense of dead-network residue still shaping live movement
- elite respectability and culture-facing access worlds that are not openly criminal but still adjacent to covert mobility and patronage logic

Use rule:
Paris should read as trailwork, access logic, and social / administrative residue, not as a generic glamorous spy postcard.

03. ET IN ARCADIA EGO
Monaco / Marietta infiltration mission. This is the glossy-surface / hidden-rot maritime-asset mission where Spook gets physically close enough to Delhardt’s covert transport machinery to recover practical intelligence from it.

04. RECEIVERSHIP
Haifa / Keter Maritime identity-confirmation mission. This is the records-and-proof mission where suspicion collapses into administrative certainty and Delhardt is conclusively tied back to Gillian Stockbridge through maritime shell structure, paperwork, and concealed beneficial-interest handling.

SUPPORT LANE NOTE
The DPRK defector safe-passage mini-arc still fits best inside or adjacent to this broader mid/late international block. It expands the conspiracy’s shipping / finance / foreknowledge dimension without displacing the Delhardt pursuit.

THEMATIC FUNCTION
NO SHELTER proves that the postwar elite still has places to hide, but those places are not magical. They are systems: offices, records, shell companies, event worlds, marinas, management layers, and the people who keep them respectable on paper.

DESIGN RULE
Treat NO SHELTER as one continuous cover-collapse arc, not as a disconnected run of stylish travel missions.
