TRAILER DIRECTION
Preferred use:
- campaign trailer track;
- emphasizes entrapment, rage, institutional pressure, and a protagonist fighting a
  structure larger than the immediate target;
- should support Permanent Record's evidence/accountability thesis rather than turn the
  trailer into a generic action montage.

ENDING / PLAYOUT DIRECTION
Preferred use:
- end-credit or playout track beginning after ONE HEAD OF THE HYDRA;
- not at the JUDGMENT DAY crash site;
- the final device-data reveal provides the correct emotional launch point.

ENDING TONE LOCK
- Stockbridge is captured alive.
- One immediate powerful branch is stopped.
- The network and its inherited/patronage-based continuation survive.
- Some names or family/succession lines already touch the rebuilding order.
- Progress is real; triumph is refused.
- The player should leave angry, unsettled, and aware that accountability must continue.

USE RULE
Do not shape the finale into "Kingmaker defeated." Stockbridge is one surviving branch,
not the whole organism. The final track should reinforce this double truth:
1. the operation mattered;
2. the struggle remains structurally larger.

PRODUCTION GOVERNANCE
- Track choice is preferred creative direction, not a licensing claim.
- If licensing is unavailable, preserve the same emotional and structural function with
  an approved replacement rather than changing the ending into a triumphal cue.
- No lyric quotation is required in source materials.
