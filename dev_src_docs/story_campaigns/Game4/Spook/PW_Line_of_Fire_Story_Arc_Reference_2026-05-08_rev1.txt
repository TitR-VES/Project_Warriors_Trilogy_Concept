PURPOSE
This page defines LINE OF FIRE, the preferred current early story arc for Spook’s Game 4 campaign Permanent Record. Its job is to reintroduce Spook through domestic postwar accountability pressure, surviving extremist violence, and chain-of-custody investigation rather than through a simple direct-action opener.

CORE IDENTITY
LINE OF FIRE is a domestic-investigation / consequence arc. It should feel like the old conspiracy is no longer hidden in the same way, but justice still has to be built one admissible piece at a time while active violent remnants try to destroy witnesses, evidence, and political survivability.

CURRENT CHAIN
1. Minneapolis State Center bombing aftermath / investigation
2. Twin Cities safe-house / staging-area exploitation
3. Chicago follow-on mission from the more urgent lead recovered there
4. Montreal bridge mission carrying the campaign toward the Delhardt / Moffat international trail

01. MINNEAPOLIS STATE CENTER BOMBING
The campaign’s early domestic consequence lane begins with the aftermath of the Minneapolis State Center bombing. Preferred function:
- establish restoration-era America as unstable, contested, and still vulnerable to ideologically motivated violence
- reintroduce Spook through scene reading, investigative follow-through, and evidence logic
- create a visible wound in the domestic reconstruction order rather than opening on an abstract intelligence brief

Presentation note:
The bombing can also support a loading-screen / pre-mission newsreel or chyron treatment, helping the campaign open on public consequence before dropping into playable investigation.

02. TWIN CITIES SAFE-HOUSE / STAGING AREA
Spook tracks bombing-adjacent suspects to a safe house or staging area in or near Minneapolis / Saint Paul. This mission should feel like exploitation of a recently used operational space rather than a full-scale warzone.

Primary functions:
- identify local preparation, movement, and support residues
- confirm that the bombing was not a lone isolated outburst
- recover the more urgent lead that sends Victor Two Bravo onward
- produce one piece of actionable intelligence that later matters to Romeo’s Game 4 campaign

03. CHICAGO FOLLOW-ON MISSION
Chicago is the next step once the Twin Cities exploitation yields the more urgent lead. The exact core target or payload remains open, but the mission’s role is already clear:
- continue the chain from domestic violent consequence toward broader network structure
- push the campaign physically and tonally out of the initial blast-investigation space
- strengthen the sense that Victor Two Bravo is following a live route, not reconstructing a cold case

Elastic-space note:
One additional mission can still be inserted between Twin Cities and Chicago later if the campaign needs it, but the current source-ready path does not require it.

04. MONTREAL BRIDGE MISSION
Montreal is the bridge that carries LINE OF FIRE into the international-pursuit block. It should preserve momentum while changing the campaign’s operating register.

Primary functions:
- move the story out of purely domestic consequence space
- connect the bombing / safe-house / Chicago line to the later Delhardt / Moffat trail
- prepare the player for the shift into NO SHELTER without making the campaign feel episodic

CROSS-CAMPAIGN TOUCHPOINT
LINE OF FIRE must preserve at least one intelligence seam that later feeds Romeo’s Game 4 campaign. Current preferred receiving lane:
- New Orleans / Lower Ninth Ward safe-house mission on Romeo’s side

Use rule:
The crossover should read as intelligence continuity, not as forced character handoff spectacle.

THEMATIC FUNCTION
LINE OF FIRE proves that postwar restoration is not a clean slide into peace. The violence has changed scale and register, but it is still political, organized, and tied to actors who believe history can be forced back into a narrower shape through fear.

DESIGN RULE
Treat LINE OF FIRE as a story arc built on consequence, evidence, and live-route investigation. Do not let it become a disconnected terrorism vignette or a generic procedural opening.
