01. CAMPAIGN IDENTITY AND END-STATE
================================================================================

Campaign title:
PERMANENT RECORD.

Core thesis:
Justice is a field operation. Spook and Victor Two Bravo must keep truth, witnesses,
evidence, chain of custody, and accountability alive while surviving networks try to
launder themselves into the postwar restoration order.

Locked end-state:
- Gillian Stockbridge survives JUDGMENT DAY, catastrophically injured, and is captured.
- ONE HEAD OF THE HYDRA follows as the device-exploitation aftermath.
- Stockbridge's capture removes one powerful surviving branch; it does not end the
  distributed organism.
- The ending is satisfactory and consequential, but deliberately non-triumphant.
- The devices reveal that names, relatives, heirs, protégés, patronage lines, or protected
  successors connected to the old network already overlap with parts of the rebuilding
  order.
- Do not turn the reveal into "everyone rebuilding is compromised." The horror is
  selective, inherited, distributed contamination and survivability.
