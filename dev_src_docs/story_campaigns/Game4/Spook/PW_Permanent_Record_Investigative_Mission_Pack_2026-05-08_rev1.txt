1. ET IN ARCADIA EGO
================================================================================

TITLE LOCK
ET IN ARCADIA EGO

ROLE IN CAMPAIGN
This mission moves the campaign from suspicion and social-theater trail work into physical contact with Delhardt’s covert maritime infrastructure. It is the shadow-asset mission, not the identity-collapse proof mission and not the endgame confrontation.

CORE PREMISE
The former Gerhana has resurfaced as the Marietta under Cayman Islands registration and a maritime-management shell tied to Keter Maritime in Haifa. The vessel matters because it functions as a discreet movement and meeting asset within Delhardt’s covert network.

ENVIRONMENT LOCK
Monaco marina / port-luxury environment at night: glossy, expensive, curated, watched, and quietly dangerous. Use polished marina surfaces, reflected artificial light on black water, dock access control, private security, and service corridors beneath a luxury presentation.

MISSION SHAPE
- covert marina approach and vessel boarding
- interior exploitation of the Marietta as a functioning covert asset rather than a villain lair
- extraction of practical intelligence: service/admin traces, routing residue, client-handling anomalies, and links that make Haifa possible later
- mission goes bad on exfil rather than on entry
- during the collapse, ship PA/audio is accidentally reactivated
- short brutal galley fight against a deceptively dangerous hostile using knives and improvised kitchen tools
- Spook finishes the hostile with NOTHING in a rapid last-inch reversal

DIEGETIC MUSIC LOCK
Teddybears — Cobra Style resumes mid-track over the yacht PA after accidental reactivation during exfil collapse. It should feel resumed from an earlier elite gathering, not cued for the player.

OUTPUT / CAMPAIGN FUNCTION
The player leaves with a compact but high-value evidence bundle tying Marietta to covert handling patterns and pointing the investigation toward Keter Maritime in Haifa.

2. RECEIVERSHIP
================================================================================

TITLE LOCK
RECEIVERSHIP

ROLE IN CAMPAIGN
This is the identity-confirmation proof mission. It collapses ambiguity around Lana Erika Delhardt by giving Victor Two Bravo hard administrative proof that Delhardt and Gillian Stockbridge are the same person operating through a maritime shell structure.

IDENTITY LOCK
Lana Erika Delhardt is the active public-facing alias. Gillian Stockbridge is the buried real identity behind the mask.

CORE PREMISE
The Marietta’s management history repeatedly points to Keter Maritime in Haifa. Keter should present as an ordinary maritime-services company handling vessel administration, charter support, customs clearing, registry/document handling, and discreet port logistics.

ENVIRONMENT LOCK
Port-adjacent Haifa, not a polished central-city tower. Preferred texture:
- older concrete commercial/light-industrial shell
- mixed-use or semi-industrial neighborhood near the port
- marine suppliers, vans, scooters, and service traffic nearby
- narrow offices, cheap glass partitions, fluorescent light, printers, filing systems, cable clutter
- a quieter, denser compliance/records layer hidden behind an ordinary front-office face

MISSION SHAPE
- low-profile approach and access under legitimate-looking business conditions
- front-office read in which ordinary paperwork starts to look wrong in pattern rather than in overt villainy
- restricted records/compliance layer as mission hinge
- proof assembled through layered cross-reference rather than one magic file
- controlled exfil under rising pressure, not a giant shootout

PROOF PACKAGE LOCK
The player leaves Haifa with enough material to collapse the mask:
- Marietta current service/admin records
- former Gerhana identity residue
- shell-routing or beneficial-owner overlap
- concealed client/admin handling tied to Delhardt
- buried metadata or admin history collapsing Delhardt into Stockbridge
- one deeper breadcrumb suggesting Keter sits inside a larger commercial-security ecosystem rather than existing as an isolated company

CAMPAIGN FUNCTION
Before Haifa, Delhardt is still a protected alias. After Haifa, the campaign moves from inference to certainty.

3. DPRK DEFECTOR SAFE-PASSAGE MINI-ARC
================================================================================

TITLE / PACK NAME LOCK
DPRK Defector Safe-Passage Mini-Arc

ROLE IN CAMPAIGN
This mini-arc links conspiracy-finance, covert shipping / sanctions-evasion, and foreign foreknowledge into a human-source extraction that feels dangerous and plausible rather than expositional. It also provides the best current live-story placement for BACKUP PLAN.

STRUCTURE
Three linked beats:
1. Seoul intermediary contact
2. South Korean-side reception / tunnel-security beat
3. compromised extraction under pressure with one defector dying after crossing and the other surviving

BEAT 1 — SEOUL INTERMEDIARY CONTACT
Preferred environment is a dense event-management / entertainment setting in Seoul, such as a K-pop concert or fan-event logistics space. The intermediary is a civilian-facing movement professional whose legitimate access to schedules, identity handling, and people movement makes them a credible bridge into a defector-assistance network.

BEAT 2 — SOUTH KOREAN-SIDE RECEPTION
Victor Two Bravo secures the South-side reception area around a secret tunnel or equivalent border-adjacent exfil route before the defectors emerge. Tone should be quiet, low-light, constrained, and tense.

BEAT 3 — HOT EXTRACTION / HUMAN-SOURCE HANDOFF
Two defectors emerge alive but damaged.
- finance / logistics / sanctions-evasion-side defector carries the critical physical package and dies after crossing
- intelligence-side defector survives and becomes the living explanatory asset

DOCUMENT PACKAGE RULE
The dying defector hands over a compact, ugly, deniable package rather than a miracle drive:
- ledger or notebook
- copied finance records
- photos or printed identifiers
- compact media with partial archive material
- ship / routing / port / sanctions-evasion markers

BACKUP PLAN PLACEMENT LOCK
This is the cleanest current story-space for BACKUP PLAN. Preferred hero/action image: Spook uses the suppressed MP7A1 one-handed, stock fully extended and braced against the inside of his shoulder, while dragging a wounded defector, teammate, VIP, or critical evidence package during an extraction-gone-bad break-contact moment.

CONSPIRACY FUNCTION
The mini-arc should illuminate:
- covert shipping and sanctions-evasion pathways
- foreign foreknowledge and quiet preparation for the PANDORA rupture
- the administrative depth and survivability of the network

DESIGN RULE
Treat the arc as a human-source recovery operation under pressure, not as a military incursion mission.
