01. CURRENT CAMPAIGN READ
================================================================================

Campaign title: PERMANENT RECORD.

Core thesis:
Justice is a field operation. Spook's Game 4 campaign is about keeping truth, evidence, witness chains, and accountability from being professionally laundered after the main war has moved into restoration.

The campaign should not invent a bigger mastermind than Goldberg or Stockbridge. It should focus on the surviving network, its escape infrastructure, its respectability covers, and the difficulty of making truth admissible, survivable, and actionable.

03. FLOW CONTROL — FUNCTIONAL LOCK
================================================================================

FLOW CONTROL is a compact pressure mission / bridge beat before …AND INTO THE FIRE.

Role:
- escalates endgame pressure without bloating the campaign
- brings La Avispa back in a professional warning function
- shows that Stockbridge-aligned infrastructure can still shape movement and kill people through deniable intermediaries
- gives Spook a grounded civilian-exposed vehicle-action set piece
- reinforces BACKUP PLAN as a contingency weapon

Preferred environment:
Civilian highway / toll-corridor environment in Argentina or adjacent South American transit space.

Best ingredients:
- multi-lane divided highway
- toll booths or lane-control chokepoint
- barrier islands and crash rails
- slow civilian traffic
- buses, compact cars, delivery vans, motorcycles
- overhead gantries, lane lights, maintenance barriers
- low admin/service buildings
- public normalcy becoming a liability

Core beat:
Victor Two Bravo is already inside a prepared engagement area before it recognizes the trap.

Phase logic:
1. Covert movement in an SUV.
2. Pattern wrongness: repeated vehicles, disciplined following distance, induced traffic slowdown, wrong-feeling roadworks.
3. La Avispa burner-call warning.
4. Choke point: toll queue / lane constriction.
5. Deniable interdiction attempt.
6. Civilian-target discrimination fight.
7. Smoke breaks the SUV through; the team does not win the firefight, it breaks contact.

Use rule:
Do not make FLOW CONTROL a long chase map or triumphant action blowout. It should be short, tense, compressed, professional, and civilian-exposed.

04. BACKUP PLAN — CURRENT WEAPON PRESENTATION LOCK
================================================================================

BACKUP PLAN is Spook's Game 4 compact suppressed PDW build.

Platform:
HK MP7A1.

Role:
Emergency secondary / compact PDW. It belongs to the moment where the operation collapses and the primary plan no longer exists.

Configuration:
- 4.6x30mm armor-defeating / armor-piercing load
- factory MP7A1 profile
- integrated folding foregrip
- top rail
- compact micro red-dot / collimator sight
- compact suppressor
- fully extendable stock
- dark, low-reflective, agency-practical finish
- no decorative markings, no vanity customization

Action-shot lock:
Spook wields the suppressed MP7A1 one-handed with the stock fully extended and the buttplate braced against the inside of his shoulder, covering an exfil route while dragging a wounded teammate, defector, VIP, or critical intel package behind him with his off hand.

Best current deployment:
Top fit is the DPRK defector safe-passage mission. Secondary fit is any later emergency exfil / break-contact sequence in Permanent Record.

Design rule:
BACKUP PLAN is not a cool-gun beauty shot. It is the weapon that appears when the op has gone wrong and Spook still has to get someone or something out alive.
