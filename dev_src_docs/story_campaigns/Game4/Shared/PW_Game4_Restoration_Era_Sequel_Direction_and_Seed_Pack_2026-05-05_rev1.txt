================================================================================
01. CORE DIRECTIONAL RULING
================================================================================

A fourth mainline Project Warriors installment is narratively viable if treated as a restoration-era sequel rather than a war-restart sequel.

The game should not:
- invent a larger hidden architect than Goldberg
- undo the gains or meaning of Game 3 merely to restart the conflict loop
- present restoration as clean, settled, technocratic, or morally neutral
- resolve the world into a neat peace-state or full-justice ending

The game should treat its central condition as the dangerous middle of restoration:
- some fronts are cooling unevenly rather than vanishing cleanly
- institutions are returning, but legitimacy is contested
- infrastructure recovery is underway, but trust remains damaged
- public truth, institutional truth, and usable justice remain in tension
- wartime actors, resistance movements, and coalition partners now disagree over what the rebuilt order should become

This direction extends the already locked trilogy arc without replacing it.

================================================================================
02. THEMATIC FRAMING
================================================================================

Locked trilogy arc remains:
Fracture. Resistance. Restoration.

Preferred Game 4 extension / sequel-pressure keyword:
Vigilance.

Use rule:
- Do not retroactively convert the trilogy into a four-term slogan.
- Treat Vigilance as the pressure concept that follows from Restoration.
- Restoration begins the rebuild; Vigilance is what keeps that rebuild from being captured, laundered, or turned into permanent emergency authority.

Why Vigilance fits more cleanly than Choice:
- the restored order remains vulnerable to capture, distortion, and partial truth
- justice can still be professionally buried after the peak war period declines
- conspiracy remnants, collaborators, and opportunists remain active
- the rebuilt order must be watched, defended, and morally checked
- the danger is no longer only invasion or occupation, but the misuse of recovery itself

Choice remains important at the character and mission level, but is too broad to function as the primary sequel-era keyword.

05. OVERALL GAME 4 THESIS
================================================================================

Project Warriors IV is not about ending the war.
It is about fighting over what restoration becomes.

Primary sequel-scale questions:
- who gets to govern after liberation
- what kind of system is rebuilt, and for whom
- which truths become admissible, survivable, or suppressible
- whether wartime legitimacy yields to civilian legitimacy
- whether the rebuild restores the republic, preserves emergency rule, or creates a new managed order wearing democratic language

================================================================================
06. THREE-CAMPAIGN ARCHITECTURE — V0.1
================================================================================

A) ROMEO — COMMAND AUTHORITY
Theme: what becomes of a resistance when it starts winning.

Core issue:
Romeo’s campaign is no longer only about defeating occupation remnants. It is about whether postwar authority becomes republican restoration, prolonged emergency rule, or something uglier disguised as patriotic necessity.

Key direction:
- AFC / post-AFC legitimacy struggle
- conflict between local armed authority and returning civilian authority
- collaborators, opportunists, and wartime actors who resist normalization
- public pressure to mythologize Romeo into a political or quasi-military symbol
- racist / reactionary / exclusionary American currents re-entering the bloodstream once common external occupation pressure declines
- tension between short-term stability and genuine civilian restoration

Important support-lane note:
André Bridges remains a strong major supporting continuity asset and should be treated as a professional counterweight to resistance mythmaking and emergency-authority drift rather than as a cameo-only figure.

B) DUCKY — COMMON PICTURE
Theme: rebuilding the system without rebuilding the lie.

Core issue:
Ducky’s campaign focuses on coalition restoration, infrastructure security, remediation of the PANDORA-damaged technical order, and widening allied fracture during the rebuild.

Key direction:
- infrastructure hardening and restoration-node defense
- remediation bottlenecks tied to damaged trust and damaged supply chains
- coalition partners cooperating tactically while diverging politically
- arguments over surveillance, autonomy, security, and resilience
- preservation of civic continuity rather than only military advantage
- selection of known-good systems versus politically expedient systems

Important support-lane note:
This campaign is the cleanest current home for Pacific-facing expansion assets, coalition partners, and regional pressure points such as South Africa, the Middle East, and selected South American nodes, provided they function as restoration-pressure environments rather than disconnected anthology detours.

C) SPOOK — PERMANENT RECORD
Theme: justice is a field operation.

Core issue:
Spook’s campaign focuses on preventing history from being professionally laundered after enough of the truth has surfaced to make accountability possible but not safe.

Key direction:
- witness protection and witness extraction
- archive recovery and chain-of-custody operations
- institutional preference for survivable truth over full truth
- legal and political pressure to narrow accountability
- protection of commissions, tribunals, and admissible evidence paths
- dismantling escape infrastructure for surviving architects and facilitators

Major antagonist / denied-escape direction:
Gillian Stockbridge is a preferred major denied-escape figure for Game 4 and should not be displaced by a newly invented master architect.

08. BUENOS AIRES / STOCKBRIDGE FUGITIVE LANE
================================================================================

Preferred direction:
In Game 4 / Permanent Record, Gillian Stockbridge attempts to disappear into Buenos Aires under the assumed identity Lana Erika Delhardt.

Cover-employment shell:
RelCom Media S.A.

Preferred role direction under assumed identity:
English-language external affairs / international communications lead.

Use rule:
- the cover should feel polished, sustainable, internationally legible, and close enough to Gillian’s genuine skills to be believable over time
- avoid making the cover too conspicuous, too glamorous, or too vague to function
- “consultant” is weaker than a defined communications-facing role attached to a small but real company

Narrative value:
This gives Spook a strong urban hunt premise built around a morally central architect trying to turn survival into permanent respectable erasure rather than overt flight.

09. RELCOM MEDIA / PUERTO MADERO ENVIRONMENTAL NOTE
================================================================================

Preferred location direction:
RelCom Media S.A. maintains its Buenos Aires executive office suite in one of the Torres Mulieris towers in Puerto Madero.

Environmental identity:
- polished luxury-residential architecture rather than a generic corporate tower feel
- controlled access, elevators, reception flow, and executive-floor privacy
- elevated waterfront views that strengthen both surveillance logic and visual identity
- a high-status atmosphere that reinforces Gillian’s fugitive-cover psychology under the Delhardt identity

Presentation rule:
The setting should work in both daytime and nighttime presentation.

Daytime emphasis:
- false calm
- respectability
- polished civic and waterfront beauty
- the contrast between public serenity and hidden moral ugliness

Nighttime emphasis:
- surveillance
- concealment
- covert pressure
- reflections, dark water, interior lighting, and expensive silence

Mission-use note:
The Puerto Madero / Mulieris environment helps prevent the Buenos Aires lane from reading as “just another big-city hunt” and gives Permanent Record a distinctive international urban texture.

Ducky Game 4, Lucas Hill expansion, Home Defense, selected Spook support beats

Functional value:
- ports, cargo corridors, routing, depots, ferries, railheads
- movement realism under Pacific pressure
