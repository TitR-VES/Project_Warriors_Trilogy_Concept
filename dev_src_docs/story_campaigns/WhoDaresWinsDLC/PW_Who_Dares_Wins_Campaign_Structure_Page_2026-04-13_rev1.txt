================================================================================
PURPOSE
================================================================================
This page turns the UK response spine into a campaign-ready mission ladder.
It exists to prevent the following continuity problem:
- Britain’s home-front survival story being developed one way
- Britain’s Boston intervention architecture being developed another way

Who Dares Wins must do the national-logic work that makes the later Boston force
package feel earned. The DLC should therefore begin in domestic shock and end at
covert transatlantic reach.

================================================================================
INHERITED LOCKS THIS PAGE MUST HONOR
================================================================================
1. Who Dares Wins is the British DLC line covering both:
   - SAS deployment operations
   - the British home front and Britain’s response to PANDORA and the subsequent war

2. The Boston-side British structure remains:
   - SAS = covert sabotage / infiltration / hostage-HVP extraction
   - SBS = covert maritime sabotage / blockade disruption / insertion expertise
   - Royal Marines (+ Royal Navy) under Lt. Dorothy Anne Vickers = later widening,
     resistance support, and practical shift in balance

3. British military CASEVAC in Boston is late-arriving, not immediate. British help
   begins as covert competence and only later widens into something Romeo can feel as
   a broader coalition infrastructure.

4. The DLC should end by making Boston possible. It should not consume the major
   Boston payoffs that belong to Romeo’s Game 3 arc.

Usage note:

================================================================================
PROLOGUE — DEAD CHANNEL
================================================================================
STATUS: Recommended opening lock
FUNCTION: Establish national shock and the campaign’s core tone.
SETTING LANE: Britain on PANDORA day; exact site flexible but must connect to military
or strategic infrastructure rather than a generic urban riot-space.

CORE IDEA
The player enters the story at the moment Britain understands that normal operating
assumptions are gone. The scene should not be maximalist disaster spectacle. It should
be disciplined confusion: dead networks, contradictory reports, misidentification fear,
fragmented command traffic, and the realization that survival now depends on preserving
specific state functions rather than restoring normal life.

BEST USE
Short playable opening rather than long cutscene. The player should do simple, concrete,
trained tasks under incomplete understanding.

KEY PAYOFFS
- Britain is hit hard.
- Britain is not untouched.
- Britain is still functioning only because professionals are holding the line.

BOSTON VALUE
This opening defines the price Britain pays before it can later matter abroad.

================================================================================
MISSION 01 — HOLDFAST
================================================================================
STATUS: Core mission
FUNCTION: Save the state, not normal life.
SETTING LANE: Sensitive military / naval / command continuity environment.

MISSION ROLE
The first full mission should dramatize Britain’s immediate triage priorities:
continuity of government, strategic survivability, early-warning continuity,
base integrity, and the prevention of cascading administrative collapse.

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- mixed interior / secure-compound fighting
- incomplete communications
- defensive redirection under uncertainty
- tense friend-or-foe ambiguity without cheap chaos

NARRATIVE RESULT
Britain preserves a critical node or process, but at the cost of accepting that
ordinary national life is now secondary to continuity logic.

LOCKED TONAL RULE
Do not write this as patriotic triumph. Write it as exhausted functional competence.

BOSTON VALUE
This mission earns Britain’s later ability to act by first proving what it chose to save.

================================================================================
MISSION 02 — TIDELINE
================================================================================
STATUS: Core mission
FUNCTION: Seal the homeland through counter-sabotage at a coastal or port-adjacent node.
SETTING LANE: Port, fuel, cable-landing, or logistics environment on the British coast.

MISSION ROLE
This is the first explicit “triage state” mission. The threat is not enemy mass assault.
It is targeted sabotage against the systems Britain still needs in order to remain a
North Atlantic actor at all.

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- maritime-industrial environment
- mixed stealth and close-quarters interception
- pressure from limited visibility, infrastructure hazards, and civilian-proximate space
- first stronger hint that the war will be won or lost through ports, fuel, routes,
  and communications rather than speeches

NARRATIVE RESULT
The player learns that Britain’s war has narrowed into a series of precise defensive
fights over throughput and survivability. The country is becoming a triage power.

BOSTON VALUE
This mission lays the first mechanical and thematic groundwork for why blockade pressure
and harbor access later matter so much in Boston.

================================================================================
MISSION 03 — GLASSHOUSE
================================================================================
STATUS: Core mission
FUNCTION: Internal counter-infiltration and proof that the enemy threat is not only external.
SETTING LANE: Domestic secure site, contractor facility, signal node, or dual-use civilian /
military workspace.

MISSION ROLE
The campaign now pivots from “the country is under attack” to “the country is also penetrated.”
This mission should expose compromised access, covert preparation, or contractor-mediated
vulnerability without turning into a full conspiracy dump.

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- intelligence-led movement rather than linear frontal assault
- room-to-room clearing, document / device recovery, partial stealth permissibility
- a sharper SAS identity emerging: professional, exact, minimally theatrical

NARRATIVE RESULT
Britain’s surviving institutions are functioning, but not cleanly. The state is alive,
not pure. That keeps the home-front story grounded and prevents easy moral flattening.

BOSTON VALUE
This mission creates the logic for later British caution and why the first Boston intervention
must be small, covert, and selectively trusted.

================================================================================
MISSION 04 — GREY WATER
================================================================================
STATUS: Core mission
FUNCTION: Shift the campaign from domestic sealing to the North Atlantic edge.
SETTING LANE: Littoral approach, offshore intercept, trawler / service vessel / surveillance
platform / disguised maritime node.

MISSION ROLE
This is where SBS becomes operationally indispensable rather than adjacent. Britain begins to
rediscover what kind of war it can still fight: exact, maritime, deniable, and high-payoff.

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- night water approach
- boarding / ladder / hull / confined-vessel movement
- navigation, timing, and route discipline matter as much as shooting
- SBS specialization is felt through how the mission moves, not only who is present

NARRATIVE RESULT
The player understands that the Atlantic is no longer an empty space between continents.
It is an active contested system that can be shaped in small ways by people who know how.

BOSTON VALUE
This is the first mission that truly begins to make submarine transit and covert transatlantic
reach feel plausible later.

================================================================================
MISSION 05 — LEE SHORE
================================================================================
STATUS: Core mission
FUNCTION: First outward special-operation with direct strategic consequence.
SETTING LANE: North Atlantic edge-space outside mainland Britain; exact geography flexible.

MISSION ROLE
The campaign must now prove that Britain can still do more than defend itself. The target
should be small, technical, and strategically meaningful: a Russian reconnaissance relay,
route-monitoring asset, logistics staging node, or clandestine surveillance position whose
removal opens maneuver space for future covert movement.

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- rough weather / exposed terrain / limited support
- longer insertion and exfil discipline
- blend of SAS strike precision and SBS access logic
- player should feel distance, isolation, and low signature rather than spectacle

NARRATIVE RESULT
Britain’s surviving strength is clarified: not mass. Not dominance. Precision.
The state is now capable of selective reach.

BOSTON VALUE
This mission is the doctrinal bridge between national survival and future overseas intervention.
It says: we can still reach.

================================================================================
MISSION 06 — NEXT OF KIN
================================================================================
STATUS: Core mission
FUNCTION: Establish the human and intelligence case for Boston.
SETTING LANE: Recovery / interception / liaison operation yielding specific Boston-facing intel.

MISSION ROLE
The campaign needs a mission where several threads converge at once:
- British nationals or intelligence-linked personnel are confirmed trapped or in jeopardy
- Boston is identified as more than just another occupied city
- the city is shown to matter to the Atlantic blockade picture and coalition leverage
- limited intervention begins to look worth the risk

This mission should not yet be the Boston extraction itself. It should be the operation that
makes that later mission unavoidable.

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- intelligence recovery under time pressure
- capture / extract rather than purely destroy
- morally loaded human stakes rather than infrastructure-only stakes

NARRATIVE RESULT
Britain’s outward turn is no longer abstract. Boston becomes the place where British interest,
human obligation, and strategic opportunity overlap.

BOSTON VALUE
This is the explicit narrative hinge that justifies the British Boston arc.

================================================================================
MISSION 07 — LONG WAKE
================================================================================
STATUS: Core mission
FUNCTION: Prepare the Atlantic lane and commit Britain to the operation.
SETTING LANE: Maritime pre-insertion shaping operation.

MISSION ROLE
Now that Boston has become strategically and humanly legible, the campaign needs the mission
that clears, confirms, or protects the covert route by which a small SAS/SBS package can get
there at all.

This is the last mission where Britain can still decide not to go.
The mission outcome should effectively make the decision for them.

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- covert maritime strike / denial action
- navigation and timing under patrol threat
- destruction of a small but load-bearing obstacle rather than a massive set piece
- player should feel how narrow the operational window is

NARRATIVE RESULT
Britain does not “return to the world stage.” It commits to one dangerous, selective,
high-value reach operation because it now has a viable lane and a reason.

BOSTON VALUE
This mission is the practical precondition for submarine-supported insertion through the blockade.

================================================================================
MISSION 08 — FIRST FOOTING
================================================================================
STATUS: Core finale
FUNCTION: Deliver the covert foothold that the Boston arc inherits.
SETTING LANE: New England coast / Boston approach space; exact insertion point remains flexible.

MISSION ROLE
The DLC finale should not become Romeo’s Boston campaign in miniature. It should do one thing:
get the British team in.

Core structure recommendation:
- transatlantic insertion under extreme low-signature conditions
- landfall / covert arrival / route off the shoreline
- establishment of first hide-site, contact chain, or observation post
- first proof that Boston resistance capacity exists and can be worked with
- ending beat that makes clear this is only the beginning

GAMEPLAY IDENTITY
- stealth-forward and tension-heavy
- minimal noise, no large-scale firefight payoff
- the most important emotional effect is: they made it

NARRATIVE RESULT
The player reaches the point where Britain’s home-front survival logic has become physical presence
inside occupied America.

BOSTON VALUE
This is the direct handoff point to Romeo’s later first contact with SAS.

================================================================================
EPILOGUE — THE OTHER SIDE OF THE OCEAN
================================================================================
STATUS: Recommended handoff scene / short playable coda / cinematic hybrid
FUNCTION: Bridge the DLC to the existing Boston architecture without consuming Boston’s major beats.

RECOMMENDED CONTENT
- SAS and SBS foothold confirmed
- first indications of task lanes already familiar from the Boston source notes:
  sabotage, extraction, harbor shaping, covert reconnaissance
- explicit understanding that this package is too small to change Boston alone
- first mention or arrival vector of the later widening British layer under Lt. Dorothy Anne Vickers
- optional final beat implying that British military support will remain covert and limited until
  a later coalition threshold is crossed

BEST END NOTE
Not triumph. Not cliffhanger melodrama.
A quiet, exact realization that Britain is now committed on the far side of the Atlantic.
