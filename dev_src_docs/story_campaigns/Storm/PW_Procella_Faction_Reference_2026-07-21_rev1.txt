SUMMARY
Procella is a distributed clandestine resistance network active across the
Russian Far East and adjacent maritime margins. It is a survival network that
became a resistance network as state machinery increasingly turned distance,
weather, infrastructure, logistics, and isolation into instruments of coercion.

Procella is not:
- a clean parallel government
- a mass public army
- a polished intelligence service
- a cinematic brotherhood
- an anti-Russian abstraction

Likely components:
- route keepers
- local technical hands
- defectors and compromised-state personnel
- port, transport, salvage, and infrastructure workers
- maritime smugglers/fixers
- relay/weather maintainers
- civilian safe-contact chains
- selected former military/security professionals

Core functions:
1. Route survival for defectors, refugees, couriers, intelligence, and equipment.
2. Infrastructure exploitation: relays, batteries, power, weather, navigation,
   generators, and dead facilities.
3. Precision sabotage that changes conditions instead of maximizing spectacle.
4. Human continuity: routes exist to preserve people, not only operations.

Operating rules:
- compartmentalization
- usefulness over ideology
- continuity over spectacle
- local knowledge matters
- fragility is constant

GEOGRAPHY
Primary lane:
- Russian Far East
- coastal towns and port environments
- remote relay/weather sites
- shoreline/offshore extraction seams
- neglected industrial and utility infrastructure

The Pacific is escape space, logistics space, surveillance space, risk space, and
contact space. It is not automatically a theater-scale naval-war campaign.

ALBATROSS
Albatross is one of Procella's most valuable continuity minds: strategist, trust
broker, hidden-route architect, and long-range pattern reader. He should remain
slightly above the frame and must not be equated with the entire organization.

DENIS / SAKHALIN
Denis represents Procella at ground level: route validation, extraction, littoral
movement, dead-system revival, and selective sabotage. If Albatross understands
the network, Denis tests whether it still exists in physical reality.

TONE
Cold, practical, restrained, weather-aware, and consequence-driven. Avoid
theatrical rebellion iconography, revolutionary speechmaking, and tidy political
simplification.
