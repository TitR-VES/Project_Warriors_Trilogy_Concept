02. CAMPAIGN THESIS
================================================================================
The Storm is a clandestine Far East lifeline campaign, not a conventional Pacific
war campaign. Its core conflict is the survival of routes, people, power, signals,
and trust under a predatory or hollow state system.

Core player fantasy:
KEEP THE ROUTE ALIVE.

Core verbs:
- move
- guide
- revive
- improvise
- sabotage
- extract
- endure

Signature materials:
- shoreline and small-boat movement
- defectors and refugee extraction
- weather and navigation data sabotage
- batteries, generators, relays, and improvised communications
- selective denial of regime coastal logistics and surveillance
- abrupt practical combat subordinate to movement and survival

Scope boundary:
Japan may appear as a limited contact seam, especially Tomakomai. The campaign
must not expand into the formal Australia/China-Pacific war lane reserved for
Home Defense.

03. PROCELLA

Procella is a distributed clandestine resistance ecosystem operating across the

intelligence service, or romantic mass movement.

Likely network components:

- compromised-state defectors
- port, salvage, transport, and infrastructure workers
- maritime smugglers and fixers
- weather/relay maintainers
- civilian safe-contact chains
- select former military/security personnel who broke with the regime

Operating principles:
- compartmentalization
- usefulness over rhetoric
- continuity over spectacle
- local knowledge over central assumptions
- constant fragility

Albatross is a long-range continuity mind: strategist, trust broker, route
architect, and pattern reader. He is not the totality of Procella and should not
be overexposed.
