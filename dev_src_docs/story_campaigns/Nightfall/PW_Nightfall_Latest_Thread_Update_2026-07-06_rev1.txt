03. DELUVIA / DAMHAVEN HAZARDOUS-TRADE RULE
================================================================================

Strict rule:
Anyone trading, transporting, or storing radiological, nuclear, chemical, biological, or similarly catastrophic material must prove safe containment while it is in or near the platform city and remains responsible for containment, monitoring, and emergency response.

Authority:
The triumvirate / council can approve, deny, or restrict entry after weighing risk.

Failure:
Failure to comply, or causing a leak / incident, is punished severely.

Current historical status:
So far Deluvia has had no major recorded catastrophic contamination incident.

Use rule:
This is not a generic “anything goes black market.” Deluvia / Damhaven can be rough, pragmatic, and trade-driven, but catastrophic material is treated as a special legal and survival category because one bad actor can endanger the whole platform city.
