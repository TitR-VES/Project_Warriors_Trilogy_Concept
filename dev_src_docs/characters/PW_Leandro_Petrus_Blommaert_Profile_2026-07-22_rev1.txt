CHARACTER LANE
Blommaert is a professional antagonist, not a supervillain or ideological zealot.
He should not be written as a mastermind.

He is dangerous because he stays calm:
- reads the breach;
- seals routes;
- moves the protected principal;
- burns compromised access;
- accepts casualties elsewhere if that preserves the protected asset;
- adapts after surviving contact.

He understands enough to know that GenCorp is dirty, but he does not necessarily know
the whole PANDORA / RIPTIDE RED architecture. His self-justification is professional,
contractual, and morally compromised rather than doctrinal.

Voice anchor, not mandatory final dialogue:
“I protect what I’m paid to protect. I don’t write policy.”

- he remains available for later Spook material.
