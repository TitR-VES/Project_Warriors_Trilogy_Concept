---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  unit/base circle).

- Dies covering Chris in the bad Phalanx armored run — the clean break

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. BACKSTORY & RECRUITMENT (LOCKED, Stefan 2026-07-18)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
After Chris quits his fabricator job at Redline Engineering (LOCKED,
Stefan 2026-07-18 — see Section 6.1), Frank reconnects with him. Frank has been working as a secure transit specialist
for Phalanx — a secure logistics and transit corporation (fictionalized
G4S / Loomis-type CIT and logistics security provider) — and tells Chris
the company has openings in the St. Louis, MO area, where Chris lives.
Frank feels the job is the right fit for someone trusted, skilled, and
dependable like Chris, and offers to put in a referral with his superiors.

Chris is hired and paired with Frank on shift — same van, many jobs
scheduled together — most likely because superiors chose to leverage
rather than discount their shared military service history and
familiarity-based efficiency and coordination.

4. DEATH (LOCKED, handoff §3.3 / §7.1)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Frank is on shift with Chris on the day their armored van is attacked.
Their failed initial evasion culminates in a daytime firefight with
several gunmen in a downtown St. Louis intersection. The van is disabled
and no longer mobile. Frank covers Chris's movement to a better engagement
position and is shot and killed in the process.

Chris's dispatch call: "This is Rhino 6, dispatch, we got a guy down
at..." + location. Aftermath: Frank is bagged; a paramedic checks Chris
with a penlight; Chris — Phalanx uniform, no cap, no weapons — repeatedly
fails to reach Carrie Nowitzki by phone, and gets up anyway. Frank's death
triggers Chris's resolve to find Carrie and sets up NO CONTACT.
