--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. CHARACTER ASSOCIATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The build name belongs to Mansell's personal rifle, not to every CheyTac M200

Generic or separately serialized Interventions remain model-name weapons.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. FINALE LOADOUT OVERRIDE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Older source material describes CRIMSON THREE as a breacher/support operator
with a suppressed Daniel Defense Mk18, Glock 19 and grenades. That material is
not silently deleted, but it no longer governs his Riptide Red finale primary.

Locked reconciliation:
- Earlier/default War Wolf role or prior-contact loadout may retain the compact
  breacher/support package where useful.
- In the Riptide Red campaign finale, Mansell carries EXTREME PREJUDICE as his
  primary named weapon.
- Any source stating that the Mk18 is CRIMSON THREE's finale primary is
  superseded and must be corrected on refresh.

This preserves prior encounter-development value while honoring the later,
specific finale lock.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. DISTINCTNESS FROM OTHER INTERVENTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXTREME PREJUDICE is distinct from:
- the CheyTac M200 hidden in the East McFarlane Island security post;
- CRIMSON TWO's separately serialized personal M200 from older encounter notes;
- generic Atlas / War Wolf precision-rifle inventory.

No ammunition count, optic, suppressor, finish, stock treatment, markings or
pickup behavior is locked by this rev1 source. Those details require a dedicated
Riptide Red finale encounter pass and must not be invented by downstream files.
