--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. BUILD IDENTITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Build name:   STAPLER
Platform:     B&T APC9K Pro-style compact 9x19mm SMG
Finish:       Black, low-reflective, practical
Role:         Compact shoulder weapon between NOTHING and GRAY GHOST.

STAPLER is Spook's Games 1–3 compact secondary / SMG build for vehicle
interiors, stairwells, hotel corridors, service tunnels, garages, ladders,
rappels and short break-contact fights. It is not a vanity weapon and does not
replace his primary rifle or sidearm.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. LOCKED PHYSICAL CONFIGURATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- compact telescoping stock
- 30-round magazines with widened rubberized baseplates
- retained detachable iron sights
- flat-dark-earth GBRS Hydra-style optic riser
- Vortex Crossfire-style micro red dot
- stock B&T side rails retained on the handguard
- low-profile forward sling point near the front-sight / top-rail area
- Nextorch WL10X-style weapon light mounted at 6 o'clock below the front
  handguard, immediately forward of the magazine well
- black Steiner Micro DBAL-style night-vision-compatible laser aiming module
  mounted on the forward rail of the Hydra-style riser
- laser pressure switch routed to a handguard side rail

Reference-image governance:
Uploaded reference photographs establish the blast-deflector visual state. The
Micro DBAL and pressure switch are canon even where a reference photograph does
not show them.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. NAME LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The short body, telescoping stock, straight magazine and widened magazine base
produce a tool-like silhouette resembling a compact industrial or fencing
stapler.

Protected deadpan line:
  "Because it stops things from moving."

Delivery: flat, without a smile or comic pause.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. CANON MUZZLE-DEVICE STATES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. SUPPRESSED CONFIGURATION
- compact B&T tri-lug quick-detach suppressor
- default for covert entries, low-signature movement and suppressed interior
  work

B. BLAST-DEFLECTOR CONFIGURATION
- B&T-style quick-detach forward blast deflector / forward-blast device
- used when stealth has failed, is no longer useful, or was never the point
- redirects blast, report and pressure forward into the engagement space while
  reducing side concussion for Spook and nearby teammates
- supports loud confined-space exits, vehicle interiors, stairwells, corridors,
  garages, toll/checkpoint interiors and forced break-contact movement

Technical-writing rule:
Do not turn the blast deflector into a magical suppressor substitute or make
unsupported real-world performance claims. The design effect is primarily
presentation, audio, handling and AI reaction.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. ATTACHMENT-SWAP GAMEPLAY RULE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The player does not freely swap STAPLER's muzzle devices through a normal
customization loop. Configuration changes occur through authored preparation or
inspect animations where the tactical register genuinely changes.

Canonical scripted use:
Mission: MAN FROM MEDAN / Operation DRAGONSLAYER, Game 1.
Location: pool-house safe beat before the breach.
Prompt: PREPARE LOUD.

One input triggers the complete sequence:
1. suppressor removed;
2. forward blast deflector attached;
3. seating click and one-beat terminal frame;
4. loud state committed for the remainder of the mission.

No music, progress bar, additional input or revert option. Post-swap inspect
shows the blast deflector and silently omits the suppressor from available
options. PW_UI_Prompt_System_Design_rev1 governs the exact six-step state flow.

Earlier optional dialogue such as "Loud it is, then" is not canon unless
separately restored.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. BLAST-DEFLECTOR AI / AUDIO EFFECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Represent the effect through animation timing, barks and short reaction windows,
not a large HUD debuff or supernatural fear mechanic.

Susceptible:
- low-discipline irregulars, opportunists, cartel lookouts, lightly trained
  security and lower-tier militia may flinch, duck or hesitate

Partially susceptible:
- trained regulars may show a fractional startle response but recover rapidly

Resistant / effectively immune:
- elite operators
- personnel using effective hearing protection
- War Wolves
- KYSC personnel shaped by Montoya Farm conditioning and CogniConnect profile
- autonomous systems, robots and drones

Robotic systems may register the sound as sensor input but do not experience
fear, freezing or intimidation.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. ONE-HANDED EMERGENCY HANDLING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAPLER permits limited one-handed emergency use while:
- rappelling;
- hanging from a ladder;
- dragging a casualty, defector, VIP or intelligence package;
- bracing inside a vehicle;
- opening or holding a door;
- using the support hand for balance or extraction.

Semi-automatic one-handed fire is difficult but viable at close range.
Full-automatic one-handed fire is limited to short emergency bursts with severe
muzzle climb, recoil-control and accuracy penalties. The suppressed state is
somewhat more manageable; the blast-deflector state is harsher indoors.

This handling grammar retroactively supports Spook's earlier campaigns and
foreshadows BACKUP PLAN's more specialized Game 4 extraction-failure role.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. INSPECT / UI RULE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Per the universal naming rule, the inspect view displays STAPLER at bottom-left.
Functional attachment labels remain world-anchored and appear only for
field-interactable items. The build receives a slightly extended compact-SMG
inspect animation appropriate to its two authored muzzle-device states.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. CONTINUITY RELATIONSHIP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAPLER establishes Spook's Games 1–3 compact-weapon and emergency one-handed
handling language. BACKUP PLAN is the Game 4 evolution: an HK MP7A1-based,
armor-defeating compact PDW optimized for extraction failure and armored contact.
They are separate builds and may coexist in continuity.
