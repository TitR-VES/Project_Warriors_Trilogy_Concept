PURPOSE
This registry records every currently identified named firearm identity across
the project, distinguishes fully specified builds from partial/recovery entries,
and points to the source that controls each specification.

Stable IDs replace bare ordinal build numbers for cross-project use. Historical
BUILD 01–12 labels remain aliases only.

0. v2.5 CORRECTIONS / GOVERNANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Retains all v2.4 corrections and source landings.
2. Adds Howler's LONG CALL as a confirmed named identity and owner association.
   Its platform and exact first-use campaign/mission remain unrecovered.
3. Records Dingo and Beaver owner-specific named-build recovery slots in the
   recovery-control page. Their exact build names are not recovered, so they are
   not counted as named identities and must not be assigned speculative names.
4. SAVING GRACE is M1911A1, not SIG P226. Purge P226 references in the SAVING
   GRACE context on sight.
5. HIGHER STANDARD, EVEN FLOW, HARDWARE INTERRUPT, SILVER LINING, DANGER CLOSE,
   and CRISIS MANAGEMENT remain confirmed named identities with unresolved
   owner/platform associations.
6. Ledger's KILLING JOKE and PUNCHLINE remain owner-confirmed; platforms and
   exact campaign placement remain unrecovered.
7. Kirill Afanasyev's EASTERN PROMISE remains owner-confirmed. "EQSTERN PROMISE"
   is treated as a transcription/typing error pending source recovery.
8. SUCKER PUNCH remains confirmed for War Within; owner/platform remain
   unrecovered.
9. STAPLER remains locked canon, not a provisional APC9K candidate.
10. EXTREME PREJUDICE remains CRIMSON THREE / Lawrence "Rip" Mansell's Riptide
    Red finale CheyTac M200; the older Mk18 remains an earlier/default breacher
    configuration, not the finale primary.
11. DEAD BLOW and DEADBOLT remain distinct locked builds.
12. DON'T TREAD retires in Romeo Game 2 Act II after the militia-recognition beat.
13. PR #22 remains a dependency for the UI/prompt-system implementation unless
    merged before this registry lands.

STATUS DEFINITIONS
- LOCKED: core identity and controlling platform are settled.
- LOCKED CORE / FLAGS REMAIN: identity is stable; lower-level implementation
  details remain open.
- NAME / ASSOCIATION CONFIRMED: preserve in registry; source recovery required
  before assigning missing platform, owner, or campaign details.
