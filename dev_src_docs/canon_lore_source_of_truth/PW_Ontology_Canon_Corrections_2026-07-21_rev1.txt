06. WAR WITHIN DLC
================================================================================
LOCKED:
- WAR WITHIN is a DLC.
- Central protagonist: Nicholas Wayne Hawthorne, callsign CROATOAN.
- Origin: Morehead City, North Carolina.
- Pre-radicalization work: civilian marine-diesel mechanic in the Outer Banks /
  Beaufort-area coastal economy.
- Skill background: marksman, militiaman, practical shooter, and former AFC covert
  saboteur during World War III.
- Radicalizing wound: his niece dies from the effects of a badly managed toxic
  coastal spill; his family also suffers the contamination.
- Hawthorne becomes disillusioned with federal authority, turns toward the New
  American Frontier, and eventually enters its elite Vanguard force.
- The campaign reveals that Vanguard operations form part of the NAF's wider
  anti-federal terror campaign.
- Hawthorne publicly assassinates Lt. Col. Stanley Abernathy during Abernathy's
  keynote address at VIATECH '31 in Washington, DC.
- The ending resolves to Hawthorne dying or being captured, depending on player
  choice.

Historical-title handling:
- AMERICAN GUERRILLA is retained as a working/source-page alias, not as a separate
  campaign and not as the controlling DLC title.

Name-spelling governance:
The existing repository sources and named-weapon registry consistently use
NICHOLAS WAYNE HAWTHORNE. The supplied correction note used “Nicolas” once.
This landing preserves NICHOLAS to avoid an unrequested identity retcon. A future
explicit spelling adjudication can supersede this rule.
