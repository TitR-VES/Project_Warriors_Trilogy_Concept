02. MACRO CHRONOLOGY
================================================================================
LOCKED BROAD PLACEMENT:
- Game 4 occurs roughly two years after the events of Game 3.
- War Within is a DLC whose events take place partly in parallel with Game 4.
- The Collapse DLC occurs roughly two years after Game 4.
- The Collapse campaign unfolds in the months preceding the eventual collapse
  of US federal authority.
- Nightfall is the last currently known chronological era.
- Nightfall begins roughly 15–20 years after Game 4.
- Nightfall occurs after the limited China–Israel nuclear exchange and after the
  formation of the Scorched Crescent.

Use rule:
These are relative chronology locks, not authorization to invent exact calendar
dates. Exact dates remain TBD unless separately locked.

================================================================================
03. NIGHTFALL / NUCLEAR WORLD STATE
================================================================================
LOCKED:
- Nightfall follows a limited China–Israel nuclear exchange.
- The exchange is limited, horrifying, and strategically specific; it is not a
  generalized global-apocalypse event.
- The exchange leaves an in-game nuclear destruction scar called the SCORCHED
  CRESCENT.
- Alternate in-game descriptive name: MIDDLE EASTERN NUCLEAR STRIKE ZONE.
- The affected scar extends from the Levant, including Syria and Lebanon, across
  the Arabian Peninsula toward Oman and the United Arab Emirates.

Source-development boundary:
Exact strike sequencing, complete target inventory, and the full scientific
fallout map remain subject to dedicated source development. The ontology should
record the locked world-state dependency without fabricating precision.

06. WAR WITHIN DLC
================================================================================
LOCKED:
- WAR WITHIN is a DLC.
- Central protagonist: Nicholas Wayne Hawthorne, callsign CROATOAN.
- Origin: Morehead City, North Carolina.
- Pre-radicalization work: civilian marine-diesel mechanic in the Outer Banks /
  Beaufort-area coastal economy.
- Skill background: marksman, militiaman, practical shooter, and former AFC covert
  saboteur during World War III.
- Radicalizing wound: his niece dies from the effects of a badly managed toxic
  coastal spill; his family also suffers the contamination.
- Hawthorne becomes disillusioned with federal authority, turns toward the New
  American Frontier, and eventually enters its elite Vanguard force.
- The campaign reveals that Vanguard operations form part of the NAF's wider
  anti-federal terror campaign.
- Hawthorne publicly assassinates Lt. Col. Stanley Abernathy during Abernathy's
  keynote address at VIATECH '31 in Washington, DC.
- The ending resolves to Hawthorne dying or being captured, depending on player
  choice.

Historical-title handling:
- AMERICAN GUERRILLA is retained as a working/source-page alias, not as a separate
  campaign and not as the controlling DLC title.

Name-spelling governance:
The existing repository sources and named-weapon registry consistently use
NICHOLAS WAYNE HAWTHORNE. The supplied correction note used “Nicolas” once.
This landing preserves NICHOLAS to avoid an unrequested identity retcon. A future
explicit spelling adjudication can supersede this rule.
