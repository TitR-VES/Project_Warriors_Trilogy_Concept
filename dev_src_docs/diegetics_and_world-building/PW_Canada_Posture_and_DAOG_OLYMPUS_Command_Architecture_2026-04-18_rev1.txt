CANADA POSTURE
Canada is a functioning but strained NATO / AEGIS-aligned partner state in the
Project Warriors world. It is not a blank space, a passive appendage of the
United States, an untouched sanctuary, or a full mirror-collapse of the United
States. It retains strategic independence and domestic priorities while still
contributing through command presence, logistics, intelligence, aerospace
warning, Arctic / North Atlantic access, humanitarian support, and limited
expeditionary capability.
