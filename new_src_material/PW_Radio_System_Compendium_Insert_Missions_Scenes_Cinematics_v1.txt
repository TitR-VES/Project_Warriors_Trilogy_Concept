RADIO SYSTEM — MISSIONS / SCENES / CINEMATICS COMPENDIUM INSERT v1

Section: Radio Deployment / Mission Packet Architecture

Purpose
This insert records the phase logic, packet sequencing, and mission-window deployment for the current Romeo campaign radio system.

Phase structure summary

Kentucky / THE RIDGE
- very early occupied-town routine: First Morning in Calloway
- early-to-mid county strain: The Redbud Vehicles
- later public narrowing under scrutiny: Washington's Wall
- late-liberation civilian reopening texture: County Breathes Again

Kentucky / FIELDHOUSE
- mid-campaign growth / shared direction: Hard Ground
- late-phase scrutiny / dispersal pressure: Washington's Wall
- late-liberation trusted local steadiness: County Holds

Boston / HARBOR
- early occupied routine / morning district crossing: First Contact
- hard-constraint / industrial-belt pressure: Open the Route
- post-BODYWORK reclaimed-function evening: First Night on the Reclaimed Row

Mission packet ledger

1) THE RIDGE — First Morning in Calloway
- very early campaign-opening civilian FM packet
- early morning
- use for opening town pass, diner/kitchen/hardware-store radio, first occupied-town civilian sound

2) THE RIDGE — The Redbud Vehicles
- late morning / midday civilian FM packet
- use for pre-recon drive, garage prep, civilian-adjacent route approach

3) FIELDHOUSE — Hard Ground
- mid-campaign burst packet
- use for safehouse prep, parked-vehicle pauses, school-adjacent spaces, quiet movement between local nodes

4) THE RIDGE — Washington's Wall
- later-strain civilian FM packet
- late afternoon / early evening
- use for Cumberland-adjacent approach, hardware-store or kitchen radio, regroup spaces

5) FIELDHOUSE — Washington's Wall
- late-phase scrutiny packet
- burst-based late afternoon into dusk
- use for relocation prep, truck loading, darkened back rooms, garage regroup under pressure

6) THE RIDGE — County Breathes Again
- late-liberation civilian FM packet
- partial reopening / damaged normality / ordinary planning returning in pieces
- use for calmer drives, shop/diner returns, later county traversal

7) FIELDHOUSE — County Holds
- late-liberation trusted local packet
- practical recovery / community maintenance / quieter local confidence
- use for school/church/garage/recovery-node revisits and late-game local recovery windows

8) HARBOR — First Contact
- morning-drive packet
- early Boston / district crossing / pre-full Elena route-authority feel
- use for apartment departure, parked-car movement, early city traversal

9) HARBOR — Open the Route
- late-afternoon industrial-belt packet
- BODYWORK branch / route and corridor pressure / public understatement
- use for approach or staging before contact in Newmarket-Widett-South Bay spaces

10) HARBOR — First Night on the Reclaimed Row
- evening post-BODYWORK packet
- reclaimed-function quiet / block-level recovery / repair-row lived consequence
- use for Van Hult revisit, workshop decompression, laundry/market/apartment spaces near reclaimed row

Deployment rules
- THE RIDGE and FIELDHOUSE are not substitutes.
- THE RIDGE is the county public voice.
- FIELDHOUSE is the trusted local community voice.
- HARBOR is the public city voice.
- Packet selection should always be checked against station role, campaign phase, time of day, geography, and whether the scene needs public voice, trusted voice, or city-systems voice.

Industrial-belt rule
The Boston industrial-belt packet family is strongest around hard-constraint and contested-city states, especially BODYWORK / Open the Route / post-BODYWORK revisit-state spaces. Reclaimed-function variants should sound practical rather than celebratory.

Open expansion slots
Kentucky
- one post-major-speech / national-ripple media packet if needed later
- one very late short-form county-normality packet if end-state texture needs another civilian pass

Boston
- one non-BODYWORK district HARBOR packet
- one deep-night safehouse / apartment HARBOR packet if a more intimate urban window is needed
- one recovery-state morning mission packet beyond First Contact if Boston's later-state routine needs dedicated texture
